1. The dimensions of a piece of Iron in the shape of a cuboid are 270 cm x 100 cm x 64 cm. If it is melted and recast into a cube. Find the surface area of the cube. [একটি আয়তাকার ঘনক আকৃতির লোহার টুকরার মাত্রা হল 270 সেমি x 100 সেমি x 64 সেমি। যদি এটিকে গলিয়ে একটি ঘনকে পুনর্নির্মাণ করা হয়। ঘনকটির পৃষ্ঠতলের ক্ষেত্রফল কত?]

  • 14, 400 cm2
  • 44, 200 cm2
  • 57, 600 cm2
  • 86, 400 cm2

Solution:

The volume remains constant when the metal is melted and recast.

Volume of Cuboid = Length × Width × Height

Volume = 270 cm × 100 cm × 64 cm

Volume = 27000 × 64 cm3

Volume = 1,728,000 cm3.

Let the side length of the cube be 's'.

Volume of Cube = s3.

s3 = 1,728,000

To find s, take the cube root. Note that \(1728 = 12^3\).

s = \(\sqrt[3]{1728 \times 1000} = \sqrt[3]{12^3 \times 10^3} = 12 \times 10 = 120\) cm.

Surface Area (SA) of the cube = 6 × s2.

SA = 6 × (120 cm)2

SA = 6 × 14400 cm2

SA = 86,400 cm2.

Answer: d. 86, 400 cm2

2. If two spheres have their radii in the ratio 2: 3, the ratio of their volumes is [যদি দুটি গোলকের ব্যাসার্ধ 2:3 অনুপাতে হয়, তাহলে তাদের আয়তনের অনুপাত কত হবে?]

  • 8 : 27
  • 4 : 9
  • 6 : 12
  • 1 : 15

Solution:

Let the radii of the two spheres be \(r_1\) and \(r_2\).

Given ratio of radii: \( \frac{r_1}{r_2} = \frac{2}{3} \).

The volume of a sphere is given by \( V = \frac{4}{3} \pi r^3 \).

Volume of the first sphere (\(V_1\)) = \( \frac{4}{3} \pi r_1^3 \).

Volume of the second sphere (\(V_2\)) = \( \frac{4}{3} \pi r_2^3 \).

The ratio of their volumes is:

\( \frac{V_1}{V_2} = \frac{\frac{4}{3} \pi r_1^3}{\frac{4}{3} \pi r_2^3} = \frac{r_1^3}{r_2^3} = \left( \frac{r_1}{r_2} \right)^3 \)

Substitute the ratio of the radii:

\( \frac{V_1}{V_2} = \left( \frac{2}{3} \right)^3 = \frac{2^3}{3^3} = \frac{8}{27} \).

The ratio of volumes is 8 : 27.

Answer: a. 8 : 27

4. Point (3, 4) lies on the graph of the equation 3y = kx + 7. The value of k is: [(3, 4) বিন্দু, 3y = kx + 7 সমীকরণের গ্রাফের উপর অবস্থিত। k এর মান হল:]

5. In a 1-kilometer race, A can beat B by 30 meters, while in a 500-meter race B can beat C by 25 meters. By how many meters will A beat C in a 100. meter race? [1-কিলোমিটার দৌড়ে, A, B কে 30 মিটারে পরাজিত করতে পারে, যেখানে 500-মিটার দৌড়ে B, C কে 25 মিটারে পরাজিত করতে পারে। 100. মিটার দৌড়ে A, C কে কত মিটারে পরাজিত করবে?]

  • 7.85
  • 7.25
  • 7.15
  • 7.03

Solution:

Race 1 (1000 m): When A runs 1000 m, B runs 1000 - 30 = 970 m.

Ratio of speeds \( \frac{S_A}{S_B} = \frac{1000}{970} = \frac{100}{97} \).

Race 2 (500 m): When B runs 500 m, C runs 500 - 25 = 475 m.

Ratio of speeds \( \frac{S_B}{S_C} = \frac{500}{475} = \frac{20}{19} \) (dividing by 25).

We want to find the ratio of speeds of A and C:

\( \frac{S_A}{S_C} = \frac{S_A}{S_B} \times \frac{S_B}{S_C} = \frac{100}{97} \times \frac{20}{19} = \frac{2000}{1843} \).

This means when A runs 2000 m, C runs 1843 m.

Now consider a 100 m race for A.

When A runs 100 m, C runs \( 100 \times \frac{S_C}{S_A} = 100 \times \frac{1843}{2000} \).

Distance C runs = \( \frac{1843}{20} = 92.15 \) m.

In a 100 m race, A beats C by: 100 m - 92.15 m = 7.85 m.

Answer: a. 7.85

6. A plane left half an hour later than the scheduled time and in order to reach its destination 1500 kilometers away in time, it had to increase its speed by 33.33 percent over its usual speed. Find its increased speed. [একটি প্লেন নির্ধারিত সময়ের আধা ঘণ্টা পরে যাত্রা শুরু করে এবং সময়মতো 1500 কিলোমিটার দূরে তার গন্তব্যে পৌঁছানোর জন্য, তাকে তার স্বাভাবিক গতির চেয়ে 33.33 শতাংশ গতি বৃদ্ধি করতে হয়েছিল। এর বর্ধিত গতি খুঁজুন।]

  • 250 kmph
  • 500 kmph
  • 750 kmph
  • 1000 kmph

Solution:

Let the usual speed be S kmph and the scheduled time be T hours.

Distance D = 1500 km.

Usual condition: \( D = S \times T \implies 1500 = S \times T \). (Eq 1)

The plane leaves 0.5 hours (half an hour) late.

Actual time available = T - 0.5 hours.

Speed is increased by 33.33...% = 1/3.

New speed (S') = S + (1/3)S = (4/3)S.

Actual condition: \( D = S' \times (T - 0.5) \)

\( 1500 = (\frac{4}{3}S) \times (T - 0.5) \) (Eq 2)

From Eq 1, \( T = 1500 / S \). Substitute into Eq 2:

\( 1500 = (\frac{4}{3}S) \times (\frac{1500}{S} - 0.5) \)

\( 1500 = \frac{4S}{3} \times \frac{1500 - 0.5S}{S} \)

\( 1500 = \frac{4}{3} (1500 - 0.5S) \)

\( 1500 \times \frac{3}{4} = 1500 - 0.5S \)

\( 1125 = 1500 - 0.5S \)

\( 0.5S = 1500 - 1125 = 375 \)

\( S = 375 / 0.5 = 750 \) kmph (This is the usual speed).

The question asks for the *increased* speed (S').

S' = (4/3)S = (4/3) × 750 = 4 × 250 = 1000 kmph.

Answer: d. 1000 kmph

7. X takes 4 days to complete one-third of a job, Y takes 3 days to complete one-sixth of the job and Z takes 5 days to complete half the job. If all of them work together for 3 days and X and Z quit, how long will it take for Y to complete the remaining work alone? [X একটি কাজের এক-তৃতীয়াংশ সম্পন্ন করতে 4 দিন সময় নেয়, Y কাজের এক-ষষ্ঠাংশ সম্পন্ন করতে 3 দিন সময় নেয় এবং Z অর্ধেক কাজ সম্পন্ন করতে 5 দিন সময় নেয়। যদি তারা সবাই একসাথে 3 দিন কাজ করে এবং X ও Z চলে যায়, তাহলে Y একা বাকি কাজ সম্পন্ন করতে কত সময় নেবে?]

  • 6 days
  • 8.1 days
  • 5.1 days
  • 7 days

Solution:

Calculate individual rates (job/day):

X: Completes 1/3 job in 4 days => Rate \(R_X = \frac{1/3}{4} = \frac{1}{12}\) job/day.

Y: Completes 1/6 job in 3 days => Rate \(R_Y = \frac{1/6}{3} = \frac{1}{18}\) job/day.

Z: Completes 1/2 job in 5 days => Rate \(R_Z = \frac{1/2}{5} = \frac{1}{10}\) job/day.

Combined rate of X, Y, Z = \(R_X + R_Y + R_Z = \frac{1}{12} + \frac{1}{18} + \frac{1}{10}\).

LCM of 12, 18, 10 is 180.

Combined rate = \( \frac{15}{180} + \frac{10}{180} + \frac{18}{180} = \frac{15+10+18}{180} = \frac{43}{180} \) job/day.

Work done together in 3 days = Combined rate × 3 days = \( \frac{43}{180} \times 3 = \frac{43}{60} \) job.

Remaining work = \( 1 - \frac{43}{60} = \frac{60-43}{60} = \frac{17}{60} \) job.

X and Z quit. Y completes the remaining work alone at rate \(R_Y = 1/18\) job/day.

Time taken by Y = Remaining work / Rate of Y

Time = \( \frac{17/60}{1/18} = \frac{17}{60} \times 18 = \frac{17 \times 3}{10} = \frac{51}{10} = 5.1 \) days.

Answer: c. 5.1 days

10. From the given figure, find tan P - cot R. [প্রদত্ত চিত্র থেকে, tan P - cot R নির্ণয় কর।]

  • 5
  • 7

Solution:

The figure shows a right-angled triangle PQR, with the right angle at Q.

Sides given: PQ = 12 cm, PR = 13 cm.

First, find the length of the third side QR using the Pythagorean theorem:

11. The simplified value of (secA - cosA)2 + (cosecA - sinA)2 - (cotA - tanA)2

  • 0
  • ½
  • 1
  • 2

Solution:

Expand each term:

1. \((secA - cosA)^2 = (\frac{1}{cosA} - cosA)^2 = (\frac{1-cos^2A}{cosA})^2 = (\frac{sin^2A}{cosA})^2 = \frac{sin^4A}{cos^2A}\)

2. \((cosecA - sinA)^2 = (\frac{1}{sinA} - sinA)^2 = (\frac{1-sin^2A}{sinA})^2 = (\frac{cos^2A}{sinA})^2 = \frac{cos^4A}{sin^2A}\)

3. \((cotA - tanA)^2 = (\frac{cosA}{sinA} - \frac{sinA}{cosA})^2 = (\frac{cos^2A - sin^2A}{sinAcosA})^2 = \frac{(cos^2A - sin^2A)^2}{sin^2Acos^2A}\)

Expression = \( \frac{sin^4A}{cos^2A} + \frac{cos^4A}{sin^2A} - \frac{(cos^2A - sin^2A)^2}{sin^2Acos^2A} \)

Combine over common denominator \(sin^2Acos^2A\):

= \( \frac{sin^4A(sin^2A) + cos^4A(cos^2A) - (cos^2A - sin^2A)^2}{sin^2Acos^2A} \)

= \( \frac{sin^6A + cos^6A - (cos^4A - 2sin^2Acos^2A + sin^4A)}{sin^2Acos^2A} \)

Use \(sin^6A + cos^6A = (sin^2A+cos^2A)(sin^4A - sin^2Acos^2A + cos^4A) = 1 \times ((sin^2A+cos^2A)^2 - 3sin^2Acos^2A) = 1 - 3sin^2Acos^2A\)

= \( \frac{(1 - 3sin^2Acos^2A) - (cos^4A - 2sin^2Acos^2A + sin^4A)}{sin^2Acos^2A} \)

= \( \frac{1 - 3sin^2Acos^2A - cos^4A + 2sin^2Acos^2A - sin^4A}{sin^2Acos^2A} \)

= \( \frac{1 - sin^2Acos^2A - (sin^4A + cos^4A)}{sin^2Acos^2A} \)

Use \(sin^4A + cos^4A = (sin^2A+cos^2A)^2 - 2sin^2Acos^2A = 1 - 2sin^2Acos^2A\)

= \( \frac{1 - sin^2Acos^2A - (1 - 2sin^2Acos^2A)}{sin^2Acos^2A} \)

= \( \frac{1 - sin^2Acos^2A - 1 + 2sin^2Acos^2A}{sin^2Acos^2A} \)

= \( \frac{sin^2Acos^2A}{sin^2Acos^2A} = 1 \)

14. Omar sells his TV at a rate of 1540 Tk and bears a loss of 30%. At what price should he sell his TV to make a gain of 30%? [ওমর তার টিভি 1540 টাকায় বিক্রি করে এবং 30% লোকসান বহন করে। 30% লাভ করতে তাকে কত দামে টিভি বিক্রি করতে হবে?]

  • 2920 Tk
  • 2480 Tk
  • 2680 Tk
  • 2860 Tk

Solution:

Loss = 30%.

Let Cost Price = CP.

15. If selling price is doubled, the profit triples. Find the profit percent? [বিক্রয়মূল্য দ্বিগুণ হলে লাভ তিনগুণ হয়। লাভের শতাংশ কত?]

  • 100
  • 200
  • 300
  • 250

Solution:

Let Cost Price = CP, Selling Price = SP.

Initial Profit (P1) = SP - CP.

New Selling Price (SP') = 2 * SP.

New Profit (P2) = SP' - CP = 2 * SP - CP.

Given: New Profit = 3 * Initial Profit

P2 = 3 * P1

2 * SP - CP = 3 * (SP - CP)

2 * SP - CP = 3 * SP - 3 * CP

Rearrange:

3 * CP - CP = 3 * SP - 2 * SP

2 * CP = SP.

The selling price is twice the cost price.

Now find the original profit percent:

Profit Percent = \( \frac{\text{Initial Profit}}{CP} \times 100 = \frac{P1}{CP} \times 100 \)

Profit Percent = \( \frac{SP - CP}{CP} \times 100 \)

Substitute SP = 2 * CP:

Profit Percent = \( \frac{2 * CP - CP}{CP} \times 100 = \frac{CP}{CP} \times 100 = 1 \times 100 = 100\% \)

Answer: a. 100

01. Evaluate \(\int \frac{1}{\sqrt{x}} \, dx\)

  • \( \sqrt{x} + c \)
  • \( 2\sqrt{x} + c \)
  • \( x + c \)
  • \( (\sqrt{x})^3 + c \)

Solution:

Rewrite \( \frac{1}{\sqrt{x}} \) as \( x^{-1/2} \).

Use the power rule for integration: \( \int x^n \, dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} + c \) (for \( n \neq -1 \)).

Here, \( n = -1/2 \).

\[ \int x^{-1/2} \, dx = \frac{x^{(-1/2) + 1}}{(-1/2) + 1} + c = \frac{x^{1/2}}{1/2} + c \] \[ = 2 x^{1/2} + c = 2\sqrt{x} + c \]

Answer: b. \( 2\sqrt{x} + c \)

02. What is the value of \(\frac{d}{dx} \ln(\tan x)\)?

  • \( \sec^2 x \)
  • \( \csc 2x \)
  • \( \frac{2}{\cos 2x} \)
  • \( \frac{2}{\sin 2x} \)

Solution:

Use the chain rule. Let \( y = \ln(\tan x) \). Let \( u = \tan x \).

Then \( y = \ln u \).

\[ \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{dy}{du} \times \frac{du}{dx} \] \[ \frac{dy}{du} = \frac{1}{u} = \frac{1}{\tan x} = \cot x = \frac{\cos x}{\sin x} \] \[ \frac{du}{dx} = \frac{d}{dx}(\tan x) = \sec^2 x = \frac{1}{\cos^2 x} \] \[ \frac{dy}{dx} = \left( \frac{\cos x}{\sin x} \right) \times \left( \frac{1}{\cos^2 x} \right) = \frac{1}{\sin x \cos x} \]

Recall the double angle identity: \( \sin 2x = 2 \sin x \cos x \).

Therefore, \( \sin x \cos x = \frac{1}{2} \sin 2x \).

\[ \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{1}{(1/2) \sin 2x} = \frac{2}{\sin 2x} = 2 \csc 2x \]

Option d is \( \frac{2}{\sin 2x} \).

Answer: d. \( \frac{2}{\sin 2x} \)

03. What is the value of \(\lim_{x \to 0} \left(\frac{1}{x \sin x} - \frac{1}{x \tan x}\right)\)?

  • 0
  • 1
  • -1
  • 6

Solution:

Combine the fractions:

\[ \frac{1}{x \sin x} - \frac{1}{x \tan x} = \frac{1}{x \sin x} - \frac{\cos x}{x \sin x} = \frac{1 - \cos x}{x \sin x} \]

The limit becomes \( \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{1 - \cos x}{x \sin x} \). This is 0/0 form.

Method 1: L'Hopital's Rule

Derivative of numerator: \( \sin x \).

Derivative of denominator (product rule): \( (1)(\sin x) + (x)(\cos x) = \sin x + x \cos x \).

Limit = \( \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{\sin x + x \cos x} \). Still 0/0 form.

Apply L'Hopital again:

Derivative of numerator: \( \cos x \).

Derivative of denominator: \( \cos x + [(1)(\cos x) + (x)(-\sin x)] = 2\cos x - x \sin x \).

Limit = \( \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\cos x}{2\cos x - x \sin x} = \frac{\cos 0}{2\cos 0 - 0 \sin 0} = \frac{1}{2(1) - 0} = \frac{1}{2} \).

Method 2: Use identities/standard limits

\[ \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{1 - \cos x}{x \sin x} = \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{1 - \cos x}{x^2} \times \frac{x}{\sin x} \]

We know \( \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{1 - \cos x}{x^2} = \frac{1}{2} \) and \( \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{x}{\sin x} = 1 \).

Limit = \( (\frac{1}{2}) \times (1) = \frac{1}{2} \).

None of the options match 1/2. Let me recheck the expression: \( \frac{1}{x \sin x} - \frac{1}{x \tan x} \).

\( = \frac{1}{x}(\frac{1}{\sin x} - \frac{\cos x}{\sin x}) = \frac{1}{x} (\frac{1-\cos x}{\sin x}) \)

Limit = \( \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{1-\cos x}{x \sin x} \). Both methods gave 1/2. There might be an error in the options.

Answer: (Result is 1/2, which is not among options)

04. Equation of the tangent drawn at point (0, 2) to the circle x2 + y2 - 2x - 4y + 4 = 0? [x2 + y2 - 2x - 4y + 4 = 0 বৃত্তে (0, 2) বিন্দুতে অঙ্কিত স্পর্শকের সমীকরণ কোনটি?]

  • x = 2
  • y = 0
  • y = 2

Yes, the point (0, 2) lies on the circle.

From the circle equation, \(2g = -2 \implies g = -1\), and \(2f = -4 \implies f = -2\), and \(c = 4\).

\( -x = 0 \)

05. If f(2x - 1) = x + 2 then what is the value of f(x + 3)? [(f(2x - 1) = x + 2 হলে, f(x + 3) এর মান কোনটি?]

  • (2x + 5)/2
  • (x + 8)/2

We want to find \( f(x+3) \). Substitute \( z = x+3 \) into the expression for f(z):

\( f(x+3) = \frac{(x+3)+5}{2} = \frac{x+8}{2} \).

06. At maximum height, what is the value of kinetic energy? [ সর্বোচ্চ উচ্চতায় গতিশক্তির মান কত?]

  • 0
  • Maximum
  • Minimum
  • None

Solution:

When an object is thrown vertically upwards, its velocity decreases due to gravity.

At the maximum height, the object momentarily stops before falling back down. Its vertical velocity at this point is zero.

Kinetic energy (KE) is given by \( KE = \frac{1}{2} m v^2 \).

07. A bird flies a distance of 8 km towards the north and then a distance of 6 km towards the south in 1 hour. What is the average velocity of the bird during the 1 hour? [একটি পাখি 1 ঘণ্টায় 8 কিমি উত্তরে এবং তারপর দক্ষিণে 6 কিমি দূরত্ব উড়ে যায়। 1 ঘণ্টায় পাখিটির গড় বেগ কত?]

  • 2 km/h towards the north
  • 2 km/h towards the south
  • 1 km/h towards the north
  • 1 km/h towards the south

Solution:

Average velocity = Total Displacement / Total Time.

Displacement is the net change in position (vector).

Let North be the positive direction.

Displacement 1: +8 km (North).

Displacement 2: -6 km (South).

Total Displacement = (+8 km) + (-6 km) = +2 km.

The positive sign indicates the net displacement is towards the North.

Total Time = 1 hour.

Average Velocity = (+2 km) / (1 hour) = +2 km/h.

This is 2 km/h towards the North.

Answer: a. 2 km/h towards the north

08. Earth's orbital radius around the sun is 1.6x108 km and rotational period is 3.14x107. What is Earth's Velocity? [সূর্যের চারপাশে পৃথিবীর কক্ষপথের ব্যাসার্ধ 1.6x108 কিমি এবং ঘূর্ণনকাল 3.14x107। পৃথিবীর বেগ কত?] (Note: Period is in seconds, likely.)

  • \( 4.7 \times 10^3 \) ms-1
  • \( 30 \times 10^3 \) ms-1
  • \( 32 \times 10^3 \) ms-1

Solution:

Assuming the orbit is approximately circular, the orbital velocity (speed) can be calculated as Distance / Time for one orbit.

Distance = Circumference = \( 2 \pi r \).

Radius (r) = 1.6 × 108 km = 1.6 × 108 × 103 m = 1.6 × 1011 m.

Period (T) = 3.14 × 107 seconds (This is approximately 1 year in seconds).

Velocity (v) = \( \frac{2 \pi r}{T} \).

\( v \approx \frac{2 \times 3.14 \times (1.6 \times 10^{11})}{3.14 \times 10^7} \)

\( v \approx \frac{2 \times (1.6 \times 10^{11})}{10^7} \)

\( v \approx 3.2 \times 10^{11-7} = 3.2 \times 10^4 \) m/s.

\( v \approx 32 \times 10^3 \) m/s.

09. Efficiency of an engine is 75% and temperature of heat sink is 67°C. What is the temperature of the source? [ একটি ইঞ্জিনের কার্যক্ষমতা 75% এবং হিট সিঙ্কের তাপমাত্রা 67°C। উৎসের তাপমাত্রা কত?]

  • 85°C
  • 840°C
  • 1087°C
  • 1360°C

Solution:

The maximum theoretical efficiency (Carnot efficiency) of a heat engine operating between a hot source (temperature \(T_H\)) and a cold sink (temperature \(T_C\)) is given by:

\( \eta_{Carnot} = 1 - \frac{T_C}{T_H} \)

Temperatures must be in Kelvin.

Temperature of sink \(T_C = 67^\circ C = 67 + 273.15 = 340.15\) K.

Efficiency (\(\eta\)) = 75% = 0.75.

Assuming the engine operates at the maximum possible efficiency (Carnot efficiency):

\( 0.75 = 1 - \frac{340.15}{T_H} \)

\( \frac{340.15}{T_H} = 1 - 0.75 = 0.25 \)

\( T_H = \frac{340.15}{0.25} = 340.15 \times 4 = 1360.6 \) K.

Convert \(T_H\) back to Celsius:

\( T_H (\text{°C}) = T_H (\text{K}) - 273.15 = 1360.6 - 273.15 = 1087.45 \) °C.

This is closest to 1087°C.

Answer: c. 1087°C

  • 30 V

  • 15/4 V
  • 4/15 V

Current (I) = 15 A.
