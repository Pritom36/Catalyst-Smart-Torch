  • solitude

11. My family consists __ four members.

  • With
  • Within
  • in
  • Of

Solution: The verb "consists" is followed by the preposition "of" when listing the components or members.

Answer: d. Of

12. Either me or pearly __ going to the party.

  • Is
  • Are
  • were
  • Will

Solution: When subjects are joined by "either...or", the verb agrees with the subject closer to it. "Pearly" is singular, so the singular verb "is" is used. (Also, "me" should be "I" in the subject position: "Either I or Pearly is...")

Answer: a. Is

13. This fills me __ relief.

  • To
  • In
  • within
  • With

Solution: The idiom is "fills someone *with*" an emotion or substance.

Answer: d. With

14. Ben is standing __ the revolving door.

  • Within
  • To
  • at
  • Of

Solution: "At" is used to indicate a specific location or point.

Answer: c. at

15. She is pointing __ the another room.

  • To
  • Towards
  • in
  • At

Solution: "Pointing to" or "pointing towards" indicates direction. "Pointing at" usually implies aiming directly at a specific object or person. "To" or "towards" fit best for indicating a room. "To" is slightly more common in this context. ("the another" is grammatically incorrect; it should be "another room" or "the other room").

Answer: a. To

Section C – [General Math]

1. A student multiplied a number by 3/8 instead of dividing it. What is the percentage change? [একজন ছাত্র একটি সংখ্যাকে ৩/৮ দিয়ে ভাগ করার পরিবর্তে ৩/৮ দিয়ে গুণ করল। ভুলের শতকরা পরিমাণ কত?] (Assuming the fraction was intended to be 3/5 as 64% is an option)

  • 34%
  • 44%
  • 54%
  • 64%

Solution:

Let the number be N. The intended calculation (Correct Value) = \(N \div (3/5) = N \times (5/3)\). The actual calculation done (Incorrect Value) = \(N \times (3/5)\).

Change = Correct Value - Incorrect Value Change = \( N \times \frac{5}{3} - N \times \frac{3}{5} = N \left( \frac{5}{3} - \frac{3}{5} \right) \) Change = \( N \left( \frac{25 - 9}{15} \right) = N \frac{16}{15} \)

Percentage Change (Error) = \( \frac{\text{Change}}{\text{Correct Value}} \times 100 \)

Percentage Change = \( \frac{N \frac{16}{15}}{N \frac{5}{3}} \times 100 = \frac{16/15}{5/3} \times 100 \)

Percentage Change = \( \frac{16}{15} \times \frac{3}{5} \times 100 = \frac{16}{5 \times 5} \times 100 = \frac{16}{25} \times 100 \)

Percentage Change = \( 16 \times 4 = 64\% \)

Answer: d. 64% (Assuming the fraction was 3/5)

2. A merchant marks his goods up by 75% above his cost price. What is the maximum % discount that he can offer so that he ends up selling at no profit or loss? [একজন বিক্রেতা তার পণ্যের ক্রয়মূল্যের উপর ৭৫% মার্ক করে রাখে। কোনো লাভ/ক্ষতি ছাড়া বিক্রয় করতে সে মার্ককৃত মূল্যের উপর সর্বোচ্চ কত ছাড় দিতে পারবে?]

  • 75%
  • 46.67%
  • 300%
  • 42.85%

Solution:

Let the Cost Price (CP) = 100.

Marked Price (MP) = CP + 75% of CP = 100 + 75 = 175.

To sell at no profit or loss, the Selling Price (SP) must equal the Cost Price (CP).

SP = CP = 100.

Discount = MP - SP = 175 - 100 = 75.

Discount Percentage = \( \frac{\text{Discount}}{\text{Marked Price}} \times 100 \)

Discount Percentage = \( \frac{75}{175} \times 100 = \frac{3}{7} \times 100 \)

Discount Percentage \(\approx 42.857...\%\)

Answer: d. 42.85%

3. If apples are bought at the rate of 30 for a rupee, how many apples must be sold for a Rupee so as to gain 20%? [যদি প্রতি ১ টাকায় ৩০টি আপেল কেনা হয়, তাহলে ২০% লাভের জন্য এক রুপিতে কতটি বিক্রি করতে হবে?]

  • 25
  • 28
  • 20%
  • 22%

Solution:

Cost Price (CP) of 30 apples = Re 1.

CP of 1 apple = Re 1/30.

To gain 20%, the Selling Price (SP) must be CP + 20% of CP.

SP of 1 apple = CP(1 + 0.20) = (1/30) × 1.20 = (1/30) × (12/10) = (1/30) × (6/5) = Re 6/150 = Re 1/25.

So, the selling price of 1 apple should be Re 1/25.

This means that for Re 1, the number of apples sold should be 25.

(Options c and d appear to be typos).

Answer: a. 25

4. A person can row 7– km an hour in still water. Finds that it takes twice the time to row upstream than the time to row downstream. The speed of the stream is: [একজন লোক স্থির পানিতে ঘণ্টায় 7– কিমি বেগে নৌকা বেয়ে যেতে পারে। স্রোতের অনুকূলে যেতে যে সময় লাগে, স্রোতের প্রতিকূলে যেতে তার দ্বিগুণ সময় লাগে। স্রোতের বেগ কত?] (Assuming 7 1/2 km/hr speed)

  • 2 kmph
  • 2.5 kmph
  • 3 kmph
  • 4 kmph

Solution:

Let speed in still water (Sb) = 7.5 kmph (Assuming 7 1/2).

Let speed of stream (Ss) = x kmph.

Speed downstream (Sd) = Sb + Ss = 7.5 + x.

Speed upstream (Su) = Sb - Ss = 7.5 - x.

Let the distance be D.

Time downstream (Td) = D / Sd = D / (7.5 + x).

Time upstream (Tu) = D / Su = D / (7.5 - x).

Given: Tu = 2 * Td.

\( \frac{D}{7.5 - x} = 2 \times \frac{D}{7.5 + x} \)

Cancel D (assuming D is not zero):

\( \frac{1}{7.5 - x} = \frac{2}{7.5 + x} \)

\( 7.5 + x = 2(7.5 - x) \)

\( 7.5 + x = 15 - 2x \)

\( 3x = 15 - 7.5 = 7.5 \)

\( x = 7.5 / 3 = 2.5 \) kmph.

Answer: b. 2.5 kmph

5. Two boats A and B start towards each other from two places, 108 km apart. The speed of the boats A and B in still water are 12 km/h and 15 km/h respectively. If A proceeds down and B up the stream, they will meet after: [108 কিমি দূরত্বের স্থান থেকে দুটি নৌকা A ও B একে অপরের দিকে যাত্রা শুরু করে। স্থির পানিতে A ও B এর বেগ যথাক্রমে 12 কিমি/ঘণ্টা ও 15 কিমি/ঘণ্টা। যদি A স্রোতের অনুকূলে ও B স্রোতের প্রতিকূলে যায় তাহলে তারা কখন মিলিত হবে?]

  • 4.5 hours
  • 4 hours
  • 5.4 hours
  • 6 hours

Solution:

Let the speed of the stream be Ss km/h.

Speed of A (downstream) = 12 + Ss.

Speed of B (upstream) = 15 - Ss.

They are moving towards each other. Their relative speed is the sum of their speeds.

Relative Speed = (Speed of A) + (Speed of B) = (12 + Ss) + (15 - Ss) = 12 + 15 = 27 km/h.

Note that the speed of the stream cancels out.

Distance = 108 km.

Time to meet = Distance / Relative Speed

Time = 108 km / 27 km/h = 4 hours.

Answer: b. 4 hours

7. There was a leakage in the container of the refined oil. If 11 kg oil is leaked out per day then it would have lasted for 50 days, if the leakage was 15 kg per day, then it would have lasted for only 45 days. For how many days would the oil have lasted, if there was no leakage and It was completely used for eating purpose? [একটি পরিশোধিত তেলের কন্টেইনারে একটি ছিদ্র ছিল। যদি প্রতিদিন ১১ কেজি তেল ছিদ্র দিয়ে বের হয়ে যায় তাহলে কন্টেইনারটি ৫০ দিন ব্যবহার করা যায়। যদি প্রতিদিন ১৫ কেজি করে বের হয় তাহলে কন্টেইনারটি চলতো মাত্র ৪৫ দিন। যদি ছিদ্র না থাকতো ও তেল সম্পূর্ণটাই খাওয়ার জন্য ব্যবহার করা হতো তাহলে কন্টেইনারটি কতদিন চলতো?]

  • 80 days
  • 72 days
  • 100 days
  • 120 days

Solution:

Let T be the total amount of oil in kg.

Let R be the rate of consumption for eating purpose in kg/day.

Case 1: Leakage = 11 kg/day. Total outflow rate = R + 11 kg/day. Lasts 50 days.

Total oil T = (Total outflow rate) × Time = (R + 11) × 50 (Equation 1)

Case 2: Leakage = 15 kg/day. Total outflow rate = R + 15 kg/day. Lasts 45 days.

Total oil T = (R + 15) × 45 (Equation 2)

Equate the expressions for T:

(R + 11) × 50 = (R + 15) × 45

50R + 550 = 45R + 675

50R - 45R = 675 - 550

5R = 125

R = 25 kg/day (This is the rate of consumption for eating)

Now find the total amount of oil T using Equation 1:

T = (25 + 11) × 50 = 36 × 50 = 1800 kg.

If there was no leakage, the oil is only used for eating at rate R = 25 kg/day.

Number of days the oil would last = Total oil / Consumption rate

Days = T / R = 1800 kg / 25 kg/day = 72 days.

Answer: b. 72 days

8. If 2 men or 3 women or 4 boys can do a piece of work in 52 days, then the whole piece of work will be done by 1 man, 1 woman and 1 boy in: [যদি ২ জন পুরুষ অথবা ৩ জন মহিলা অথবা ৪ জন বালক একটি কাজ ৫২ দিনে করতে পারে, তাহলে সম্পূর্ণ কাজটি ১ জন পুরুষ, ১ জন মহিলা এবং ১ জন বালক কত দিনে পারবে?]

  • 48 days
  • 36 days
  • 45 days
  • 50 days

Solution:

Let the work rates be M, W, B for one man, one woman, one boy respectively.

From the given information, the total work can be done by:

2 men in 52 days => Rate of 2 men = 1/52 => 2M = 1/52 => M = 1/104 job/day.

3 women in 52 days => Rate of 3 women = 1/52 => 3W = 1/52 => W = 1/156 job/day.

4 boys in 52 days => Rate of 4 boys = 1/52 => 4B = 1/52 => B = 1/208 job/day.

We need to find the time taken by 1 man, 1 woman, and 1 boy working together.

Combined rate = M + W + B

Combined rate = 1/104 + 1/156 + 1/208

Find LCM of 104, 156, 208. (104 = 8*13, 156 = 12*13, 208 = 16*13). LCM = 16*3*13 = 48*13 = 624.

Combined rate = \( \frac{6}{624} + \frac{4}{624} + \frac{3}{624} = \frac{6+4+3}{624} = \frac{13}{624} \)

Simplify: \( \frac{13}{13 \times 48} = \frac{1}{48} \) job/day.

Time taken together = 1 / Combined rate = 1 / (1/48) = 48 days.

Answer: a. 48 days

The total quantity of water in the new mixture is: [একটি মিশ্রণে দুধ ও পানির অনুপাত ৭:৫। যখন মিশ্রণে ১৫ লিটার পানি যোগ করা হয়, তখন নতুন মিশ্রণে দুধ ও পানির অনুপাত ৭:৮ হয়। নতুন মিশ্রণে পানির মোট পরিমাণ কত?]

  • 60 liters
  • 96 liters

New quantity of water = 5x + 15.

\( \frac{\text{New Milk}}{\text{New Water}} = \frac{7x}{5x + 15} = \frac{7}{8} \)

Divide by 7: \( 8x = 5x + 15 \)

\( 3x = 15 \)

\( x = 5 \)

The question asks for the total quantity of water in the *new* mixture.

New quantity of water = 5x + 15 = 5(5) + 15 = 25 + 15 = 40 liters.

4. What is the equation of the diameter of the circle x2 + y2 - 12x + 4y + 6 = 0?

  • x = y
  • x + 3y = 0
  • 3x + 2y = 0

Solution:

A diameter of a circle is any straight line that passes through the center of the circle.

First, find the center of the circle \(x^2 + y^2 - 12x + 4y + 6 = 0\).

The general equation is \(x^2 + y^2 + 2gx + 2fy + c = 0\), where the center is \((-g, -f)\).

Comparing coefficients:

\( 2g = -12 \implies g = -6 \)

\( 2f = 4 \implies f = 2 \)

The center of the circle is \((-g, -f) = (-(-6), -2) = (6, -2)\).

Now, we need to check which of the given line equations is satisfied by the point (6, -2).

a. x + y = 0 => 6 + (-2) = 4 ≠ 0.

b. x = y => 6 ≠ -2.

c. x + 3y = 0 => 6 + 3(-2) = 6 - 6 = 0. This is satisfied.

d. 3x + 2y = 0 => 3(6) + 2(-2) = 18 - 4 = 14 ≠ 0.

The line \(x + 3y = 0\) passes through the center (6, -2) and is therefore an equation of a diameter.

Answer: c. x + 3y = 0

06. According to the Aufbau principle, which order is correct given below? [আউফবাউ নীতি অনুসারে, নিচের কোন ক্রমটি সঠিক?]

  • 3d<4s<4p
  • 4d<5s<5p
  • 5s<5p<4d

Solution:

The Aufbau principle states that electrons fill atomic orbitals starting from the lowest available energy levels before filling higher levels. The energy order is generally determined by the (n+l) rule, and for equal (n+l), the lower n value has lower energy.

  • 4s: n=4, l=0 -> n+l = 4
  • 3d: n=3, l=2 -> n+l = 5
  • 4p: n=4, l=1 -> n+l = 5

Comparing 3d and 4p (both n+l=5), 3d has lower n, so 3d < 4p.

Comparing 4s (n+l=4) and 3d (n+l=5), 4s < 3d.

So the order is 4s < 3d < 4p.

Let's check other options:

  • b. 3d<4s<4p: Incorrect (4s < 3d).
  • c. 4d<5s<5p: 4d (n+l=4+2=6), 5s (n+l=5+0=5), 5p (n+l=5+1=6). Order should be 5s < 4d < 5p. Incorrect.
  • d. 5s<5p<4d: Incorrect (4d < 5p).

    • The correct order based on Aufbau principle is 4s < 3d < 4p.

    Answer: a. 4s<3d<4p

    • NHCOCH3

    Solution:

    In electrophilic aromatic substitution, substituents on the benzene ring direct incoming electrophiles to specific positions (ortho, para, or meta). "Meta indicators" usually refers to meta-directing groups.

    • -NO2 (Nitro group): Strongly electron-withdrawing, deactivating, and meta-directing.
    • -Br (Bromo group): Halogen, weakly deactivating, ortho/para-directing due to lone pairs capable of resonance donation.
    • -NH2 (Amino group): Strongly electron-donating, activating, ortho/para-directing.
    • -NHCOCH3 (Acetamido group): Moderately electron-donating (less than -NH2 due to withdrawing carbonyl), activating, ortho/para-directing.

    The nitro group (-NO2) is the meta-directing group among the options.

    Answer: a. -NO2

    08. Which is the major source of CO pollution? [CO দূষণের প্রধান উৎস কোনটি?]

    • Deforestation
    • Automobile exhaust
    • Forest fire
    • both a and b

    Solution:

    Carbon monoxide (CO) is primarily produced by the incomplete combustion of carbon-containing fuels. The major source of CO pollution, especially in urban areas, is exhaust from automobiles (internal combustion engines). Forest fires also produce CO, but automobile exhaust is generally considered the dominant anthropogenic source globally. Deforestation is not a direct source of CO.

    Answer: b. Automobile exhaust

    09. Which is not an oxidizing agent? [ কোনটি জারক নয়?]

    • CO
    • MnO2
    • H2
    • H2O2

    Solution:

    An oxidizing agent is a substance that accepts electrons in a redox reaction, causing oxidation in another substance while being reduced itself.

    • CO (Carbon Monoxide): Can act as a reducing agent (e.g., in blast furnaces, C gets oxidized to CO2). It's not typically considered an oxidizing agent.
    • MnO2 (Manganese Dioxide): A common oxidizing agent (Mn is in +4 state, can be reduced, e.g., to +2).
    • H2 (Hydrogen gas): Typically acts as a reducing agent (H gets oxidized from 0 to +1).
    • H2O2 (Hydrogen Peroxide): Can act as both an oxidizing agent (O gets reduced from -1 to -2) and a reducing agent (O gets oxidized from -1 to 0). It is often used as an oxidizing agent.

    Both CO and H2 primarily act as reducing agents. The question asks which is *not* an oxidizing agent. Both fit this description more strongly than MnO2 or H2O2. However, CO is a very common reducing agent. Assuming H2 (option c) is intended as Hydrogen gas.

    Answer: a. CO (or c. H2)

    10. One mole of electricity is- [ এক মোল বিদ্যুৎ হল-]

    • 1 coulomb electricity
    • 1 faraday electricity
    • 0.1 coulomb electricity
    • 0.1 faraday electricity

    Solution:

    "One mole of electricity" refers to the charge carried by one mole of electrons. This quantity is defined as one Faraday (F).

    1 Faraday ≈ 96,485 coulombs.

    It represents the amount of charge required to deposit or liberate one mole of a substance that undergoes a one-electron change in an electrochemical reaction.

    Answer: b. 1 faraday electricity
