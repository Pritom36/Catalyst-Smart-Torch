1. If a person takes 1000-taka loan for 5 years and pays 300 taka Interest, what is her Interest rate? [যদি একজন ব্যক্তি 5 বছরের জন্য 1000 টাকা ঋণ নেয় এবং 300 টাকা সুদ দেয়, তার সুদের হার কত?]

  • 5%
  • 7%
  • 8%
  • 6%

Solution:

Using the Simple Interest formula: SI = P × R × T.

Principal (P) = 1000 taka.

Time (T) = 5 years.

Simple Interest (SI) = 300 taka.

Rate (R) = ?

300 = 1000 × R × 5

300 = 5000 × R

R = 300 / 5000 = 3 / 50 = 6 / 100 = 0.06.

To express the rate as a percentage, multiply by 100:

Rate = 0.06 × 100 = 6%.

Answer: d. 6%

2. Baurcan completes a certain journey by a bus. If he covered 40% of the distance at the speed of 40km/h. 30% of the distance at 60km/h and the remaining of the distance at 20kmph, his average speed is: [বাউরচান বাসে করে একটি নির্দিষ্ট যাত্রা সম্পন্ন করে। যদি সে 40 কিমি/ঘন্টা গতিতে 40% দূরত্ব, 30% দূরত্ব 60 কিমি/ঘন্টা এবং বাকি দূরত্ব 20 কিমি/ঘন্টা বেগে অতিক্রম করে, তাহলে তার গড় গতি হল:]

  • 33.3 km/h
  • 69 km/h
  • 40 km/h
  • 55 km/h

Solution:

Average speed = Total Distance / Total Time.

Let the total distance be D km.

Part 1: Distance \(d_1 = 0.40 D\). Speed \(s_1 = 40\) km/h.

Time \(t_1 = d_1 / s_1 = (0.40 D) / 40 = D / 100\) hours.

Part 2: Distance \(d_2 = 0.30 D\). Speed \(s_2 = 60\) km/h.

Time \(t_2 = d_2 / s_2 = (0.30 D) / 60 = D / 200\) hours.

Part 3: Remaining distance \(d_3 = D - d_1 - d_2 = D - 0.40 D - 0.30 D = 0.30 D\).

Speed \(s_3 = 20\) km/h.

Time \(t_3 = d_3 / s_3 = (0.30 D) / 20 = 3D / 200\) hours.

Total Distance = D.

Total Time = \(t_1 + t_2 + t_3 = \frac{D}{100} + \frac{D}{200} + \frac{3D}{200}\).

Total Time = \( \frac{2D}{200} + \frac{D}{200} + \frac{3D}{200} = \frac{2D + D + 3D}{200} = \frac{6D}{200} = \frac{3D}{100} \) hours.

Average Speed = \( \frac{\text{Total Distance}}{\text{Total Time}} = \frac{D}{3D / 100} \)

Average Speed = \( D \times \frac{100}{3D} = \frac{100}{3} \) km/h.

Average Speed = 33.33... km/h.

Answer: a. 33.3 km/h

3. Median of the following data set: 2,3,2,4,5,7,5,4,8,5- [ নিম্নলিখিত ডেটা সেটের মধ্যক: 2,3,2,4,5,7,5,4,8,5-] (Assuming the list ends at 5)

  • 5.5
  • 4
  • 4.5
  • 5

Solution:

Data set: 2, 3, 2, 4, 5, 7, 5, 4, 8, 5.

First, sort the data in ascending order:

2, 2, 3, 4, 4, 5, 5, 5, 7, 8.

Count the number of terms (n). There are 10 terms.

Since n is even, the median is the average of the two middle terms, which are at the n/2 and (n/2)+1 positions.

Positions = 10/2 = 5th and (10/2)+1 = 6th.

The 5th term is 4.

The 6th term is 5.

Median = (5th term + 6th term) / 2 = (4 + 5) / 2 = 9 / 2 = 4.5.

Answer: c. 4.5

4. From a pack of 52 cards, one card is drawn at random. What is the probability that the card is a ten or a spade? [52টি তাসের একটি প্যাকেট থেকে, একটি তাস এলোমেলোভাবে টানা হয়। কার্ডটি দশ বা একটি স্পেড হওয়ার সম্ভাবনা কত?]

  • 4/13
  • 1/4
  • 1/13
  • 1/26

Solution:

Let T be the event of drawing a Ten.

Let S be the event of drawing a Spade.

Total cards = 52.

Number of Tens = 4 (Ten of Spades, Hearts, Diamonds, Clubs).

P(T) = 4/52 = 1/13.

Number of Spades = 13.

P(S) = 13/52 = 1/4.

The event "Ten and Spade" is drawing the Ten of Spades. Number of (T ∩ S) = 1.

P(T ∩ S) = 1/52.

We want the probability of drawing a Ten OR a Spade, P(T ∪ S).

P(T ∪ S) = P(T) + P(S) - P(T ∩ S)

P(T ∪ S) = (4/52) + (13/52) - (1/52)

P(T ∪ S) = (4 + 13 - 1) / 52 = 16 / 52.

Simplify 16/52 by dividing by 4:

P(T ∪ S) = 4 / 13.

Answer: a. 4/13

5. Store gives a discount of 20% on a certain product and then gives another 10% discount on the product so how much is the total discount percentage? [স্টোর একটি নির্দিষ্ট পণ্যের উপর 20% ডিসকাউন্ট দেয় এবং তারপর পণ্যটির উপর আরও 10% ডিসকাউন্ট দেয় তাহলে মোট ডিসকাউন্ট শতাংশ কত?]

  • 30%
  • 33%
  • 27%
  • 28%

Solution:

Let the original price be P = 100.

First discount = 20% of P = 0.20 × 100 = 20.

Price after first discount = 100 - 20 = 80.

Second discount = 10% *on the discounted price* = 10% of 80 = 0.10 × 80 = 8.

Final Price = Price after first discount - Second discount = 80 - 8 = 72.

Total Discount Amount = Original Price - Final Price = 100 - 72 = 28.

Total Discount Percentage = \( \frac{\text{Total Discount Amount}}{\text{Original Price}} \times 100 \)

Total Discount Percentage = \( \frac{28}{100} \times 100 = 28\% \).

Alternatively, using successive discount formula: \( D_{total} = D_1 + D_2 - \frac{D_1 D_2}{100} \) (where discounts are percentages).

\( D_{total} = 20 + 10 - \frac{20 \times 10}{100} = 30 - \frac{200}{100} = 30 - 2 = 28\% \).

Answer: d. 28%

7. The value of (1+ cotx- cosecx) (1 + tanx + secx) is equal to? [(1+ cotx- cosecx) (1 + tanx + secx) এর মান কত?]

  • 1
  • 2
  • -1

Solution:

Convert everything to sinx and cosx:

First bracket: \( 1 + \frac{\cos x}{\sin x} - \frac{1}{\sin x} = \frac{\sin x + \cos x - 1}{\sin x} \)

Second bracket: \( 1 + \frac{\sin x}{\cos x} + \frac{1}{\cos x} = \frac{\cos x + \sin x + 1}{\cos x} \)

Multiply the two brackets:

\( \frac{(\sin x + \cos x - 1)(\sin x + \cos x + 1)}{\sin x \cos x} \)

The numerator is in the form \((a - b)(a + b) = a^2 - b^2\), where \(a = \sin x + \cos x\) and \(b = 1\).

Numerator = \( (\sin x + \cos x)^2 - 1^2 \)

Numerator = \( (\sin^2 x + \cos^2 x + 2 \sin x \cos x) - 1 \)

Since \( \sin^2 x + \cos^2 x = 1 \):

Numerator = \( (1 + 2 \sin x \cos x) - 1 = 2 \sin x \cos x \).

So the expression becomes:

\( \frac{2 \sin x \cos x}{\sin x \cos x} \)

Assuming \( \sin x \neq 0 \) and \( \cos x \neq 0 \), we can cancel \( \sin x \cos x \):

Value = 2.

Answer: b. 2

9. Anik complete a work In 30 days. Anik and Shafik can complete the work together In 15 days. Anik and Saikat together can complete the work in 20 days. How long would it take for Shafik and Saikat together to complete the work? [অনিক একটি কাজ 30 দিনে সম্পন্ন করে। অনিক ও শফিক একসাথে কাজটি 15 দিনে সম্পন্ন করতে পারে। অনিক ও সৈকত একসাথে কাজটি 20 দিনে সম্পন্ন করতে পারে। শফিক ও সৈকত একসাথে কাজটি সম্পন্ন করতে কত সময় লাগবে?]

  • 10 days
  • 30 days
  • 20 days
  • 15 days

Solution:

Let rates be R_A, R_S, R_K for Anik, Shafik, Saikat respectively (jobs/day).

R_A = 1/30.

R_A + R_S = 1/15.

R_A + R_K = 1/20.

We need to find the time for Shafik and Saikat together, which means we need their combined rate R_S + R_K.

10. Out of 65 cars on a car lot, 45 have air conditioning, 30 have power windows and 12 have both air conditioning and power windows. How many of the cars on the lot have neither air conditioning nor power windows? [একটি কার লটে 65টি গাড়ির মধ্যে 45টিতে এয়ার কন্ডিশনার, 30টিতে পাওয়ার উইন্ডো এবং 12টিতে এয়ার কন্ডিশনার ও পাওয়ার উইন্ডো উভয়ই আছে। লটের কতগুলি গাড়িতে এয়ার কন্ডিশনার বা পাওয়ার উইন্ডো নেই?]

  • 5
  • 10
  • 4
  • 2

Solution:

Let AC be the set of cars with air conditioning.

Let PW be the set of cars with power windows.

Total cars = 65.

|AC| = 45.

|PW| = 30.

|AC ∩ PW| = 12 (cars with both).

Number of cars with either AC or PW or both, |AC ∪ PW|, is:

|AC ∪ PW| = |AC| + |PW| - |AC ∩ PW|

|AC ∪ PW| = 45 + 30 - 12 = 75 - 12 = 63.

So, 63 cars have at least one of the features.

Number of cars with neither feature = Total cars - |AC ∪ PW|

Number with neither = 65 - 63 = 2.

Answer: d. 2

14. The seventh term of a geometric sequence with ratio 2 is 448. What is the first term of the sequence? [অনুপাত 2 সহ একটি জ্যামিতিক অনুক্রমের সপ্তম পদ 448। অনুক্রমের প্রথম পদটি কী?]

  • 7
  • 8
  • 9
  • 11

Solution:

In a geometric sequence, the nth term (\(a_n\)) is given by \( a_n = a_1 \times r^{(n-1)} \), where \(a_1\) is the first term and r is the common ratio.

Given:

Common ratio (r) = 2.

Seventh term (\(a_7\)) = 448.

n = 7.

Substitute into the formula:

\( a_7 = a_1 \times r^{(7-1)} \)

\( 448 = a_1 \times 2^6 \)

\( 2^6 = 64 \).

\( 448 = a_1 \times 64 \)

\( a_1 = 448 / 64 \)

\( a_1 = 7 \).

The first term is 7.

Answer: a. 7

15. The area of triangle ∆ABC is \(\frac{1}{4}(a^2 + b^2)\). What are the angles of the triangle? [ ত্রিভুজ ∆ABC এর ক্ষেত্রফল \(\frac{1}{4}(a^2 + b^2)\)। ত্রিভুজের কোণগুলো কী কী?]

  • 90°, 45°, 45°
  • 45°, 60°, 45°
  • 60°, 60°, 60°
  • 30°, 60°, 90°

Solution:

The standard formula for the area of a triangle is \( \text{Area} = \frac{1}{2} ab \sin C \), where C is the angle between sides a and b.

We are given Area = \( \frac{1}{4}(a^2 + b^2) \).

So, \( \frac{1}{2} ab \sin C = \frac{1}{4}(a^2 + b^2) \).

\( 2 ab \sin C = a^2 + b^2 \).

\( \sin C = \frac{a^2 + b^2}{2ab} \).

We also know by the AM-GM inequality that for positive a, b: \( \frac{a^2 + b^2}{2} \ge \sqrt{a^2 b^2} = ab \), so \( a^2 + b^2 \ge 2ab \).

Therefore, \( \frac{a^2 + b^2}{2ab} \ge 1 \).

Since the maximum value of sin C is 1, the only possibility for \( \sin C = \frac{a^2 + b^2}{2ab} \) to hold is if \( \sin C = 1 \) and \( \frac{a^2 + b^2}{2ab} = 1 \).

1. \( \sin C = 1 \) implies \( C = 90^\circ \).

2. \( \frac{a^2 + b^2}{2ab} = 1 \) implies \( a^2 + b^2 = 2ab \implies a^2 - 2ab + b^2 = 0 \implies (a - b)^2 = 0 \implies a = b \).

So, the triangle must be a right-angled triangle (C=90°) and isosceles (a=b).

If C=90° and a=b, the other two angles A and B must be equal and add up to 90° (since A+B+C=180°).

A = B = 90° / 2 = 45°.

The angles are 90°, 45°, 45°.

Answer: a. 90°, 45°, 45°

01. If \( y = \sqrt{sinx + \sqrt{sinx + \sqrt{sinx + ........\infty}}} \) then dy/dx = ?

02. What will be the area of the region enclosed by x = y2, y = x - 2, integrating with respect to y? [x = y2, y = x - 2 রেখা দ্বারা আবদ্ধ অঞ্চলের ক্ষেত্রফল কত হবে? (y-এর সাপেক্ষে যোগজীকরণ করে)]

  • 10/3
  • 9/2
  • 2/9
  • 2

Solution:

We need to integrate with respect to y. This means expressing the boundaries as functions of y and integrating along the y-axis.

Curve 1: \( x = y^2 \) (parabola opening right).

Curve 2: \( y = x - 2 \implies x = y + 2 \) (line).

Find the intersection points by setting the x values equal:

\( y^2 = y + 2 \)

\( y^2 - y - 2 = 0 \)

Factor the quadratic: \( (y - 2)(y + 1) = 0 \).

The intersection points occur at y = -1 and y = 2.

Now determine which function gives the larger x-value (right boundary) and which gives the smaller x-value (left boundary) in the interval \(y \in [-1, 2]\).

Test y=0: \(x = y^2 = 0\). \(x = y + 2 = 2\). So, \(y+2\) is the right boundary and \(y^2\) is the left boundary.

Area = \( \int_{y_{lower}}^{y_{upper}} [x_{right}(y) - x_{left}(y)] \, dy \)

Area = \( \int_{-1}^2 [(y+2) - (y^2)] \, dy \)

Area = \( \int_{-1}^2 (-y^2 + y + 2) \, dy \)

Integrate:

Area = \( \left[ -\frac{y^3}{3} + \frac{y^2}{2} + 2y \right]_{-1}^2 \)

Evaluate at limits:

Upper limit (y=2): \( -\frac{2^3}{3} + \frac{2^2}{2} + 2(2) = -\frac{8}{3} + \frac{4}{2} + 4 = -\frac{8}{3} + 2 + 4 = 6 - \frac{8}{3} = \frac{18-8}{3} = \frac{10}{3} \)

Lower limit (y=-1): \( -\frac{(-1)^3}{3} + \frac{(-1)^2}{2} + 2(-1) = -\frac{-1}{3} + \frac{1}{2} - 2 = \frac{1}{3} + \frac{1}{2} - 2 = \frac{2+3-12}{6} = -\frac{7}{6} \)

Area = (Value at upper limit) - (Value at lower limit)

Area = \( \frac{10}{3} - (-\frac{7}{6}) = \frac{10}{3} + \frac{7}{6} = \frac{20}{6} + \frac{7}{6} = \frac{27}{6} = \frac{9}{2} \)

Answer: b. 9/2

03. Which of the following locus is equidistant from (a, 0) and (-a, 0)? [ নিচের কোন সঞ্চারপথ (a, 0) ও (-a, 0) বিন্দুদ্বয় থেকে সমদূরবর্তী?]

  • x = 0
  • x = a
  • y = a

A line perpendicular to the x-axis is a vertical line.

The vertical line passing through the origin (0, 0) is the y-axis, which has the equation x = 0.

04. Which one of the following is equivalent to 4 < x < 10? [নিচের কোনটি 4 < x < 10 এর সমতুল্য?]

  • |x - 7| < 3
  • |x + 3| > 7
  • |x + 3| < 7

Solution:

The inequality \( 4 < x < 10 \) represents all numbers x between 4 and 10.

The center of this interval is \( (4 + 10) / 2 = 14 / 2 = 7 \).

The distance from the center (7) to either endpoint (4 or 10) is 3 (i.e., 7-4 = 3 and 10-7 = 3).

The inequality can be expressed as the set of points x whose distance from the center (7) is less than 3.

In absolute value notation, this is written as: \( |x - \text{center}| < \text{distance} \)

So, \( |x - 7| < 3 \).

  • d. |x + 3| < 7 => -7 < x + 3 < 7 => -10 < x < 4. Incorrect.

    05. What is the value of \(\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin^{-1} 3x}{x}\)?

    06. What is the law of velocity of Galileo's law of falling bodies? [গ্যালিলিওর পতনশীল বস্তুর বেগের সূত্র কোনটি?]

    • v α a
    • v α t
    • v α t2
    • p α v

    Solution:

    Galileo's laws for freely falling bodies (starting from rest, neglecting air resistance):

    1. Distance fallen (s) is proportional to the square of the time (\(s \propto t^2\)).
    2. The velocity (v) acquired is directly proportional to the time (\(v \propto t\)).
    3. The velocity squared is proportional to the distance fallen (\(v^2 \propto s\)).

    These correspond to the kinematic equations with initial velocity u=0 and constant acceleration a=g:

    1. \( s = ut + \frac{1}{2}at^2 \implies s = \frac{1}{2}gt^2 \implies s \propto t^2 \)
    2. \( v = u + at \implies v = gt \implies v \propto t \)
    3. \( v^2 = u^2 + 2as \implies v^2 = 2gs \implies v^2 \propto s \)

    The law relating velocity and time is \( v \propto t \).

    Answer: b. v α t

    07. For which condition the vectors A, B and C will be coplanar? [ কোন শর্তাধীনে A, B এবং C ভেক্টর সমতলীয় হবে?]

    • A.(B.C) = 0
    • A.(B x C) = 0
    • c.Ax (B.C) = 0
    • d.Ax (B x C) = 0

    Solution:

    Three vectors \(\vec{A}\), \(\vec{B}\), and \(\vec{C}\) are coplanar if their scalar triple product is zero.

    The scalar triple product can be calculated as the dot product of one vector with the cross product of the other two: \( \vec{A} \cdot (\vec{B} \times \vec{C}) \).

    The geometric interpretation is that the scalar triple product represents the volume of the parallelepiped formed by the three vectors. If the vectors are coplanar, the volume is zero.

    Therefore, the condition for coplanarity is \( \vec{A} \cdot (\vec{B} \times \vec{C}) = 0 \).

    (Note: Options c and d have typos, likely intending cross product '×').

    Answer: b. A.(B x C) = 0

    08. For a projectile if maximum horizontal range = R then what is the maximum height? [একটি প্রাসের জন্য যদি সর্বোচ্চ অনুভূমিক পাল্লা = R হয় তবে সর্বোচ্চ উচ্চতা কত?]

    • R/2
    • R
    • 2R
    • R/4

    Solution:

    The maximum horizontal range (R_max) for a projectile launched with initial velocity \(v_0\) occurs when the launch angle \(\theta = 45^\circ\).

    Formula for Range: \( R = \frac{v_0^2 \sin(2\theta)}{g} \).

    Maximum Range (\(\theta=45^\circ\)): \( R_{max} = \frac{v_0^2 \sin(90^\circ)}{g} = \frac{v_0^2}{g} \).

    Formula for Maximum Height: \( H = \frac{v_0^2 \sin^2\theta}{2g} \).

    We need the maximum height (H) when the range is maximum (i.e., when \(\theta = 45^\circ\)).

    Substitute \(\theta = 45^\circ\) into the height formula:

    \( H (\text{at } \theta=45^\circ) = \frac{v_0^2 \sin^2(45^\circ)}{2g} \)

    Since \( \sin(45^\circ) = 1/\sqrt{2} \), \( \sin^2(45^\circ) = (1/\sqrt{2})^2 = 1/2 \).

    \( H = \frac{v_0^2 (1/2)}{2g} = \frac{v_0^2}{4g} \).

    Now relate this height H to the maximum range \(R_{max} = v_0^2 / g\).

    \( H = \frac{1}{4} \left( \frac{v_0^2}{g} \right) = \frac{1}{4} R_{max} \).

    So, the maximum height achieved when the range is maximum is R/4 (where R denotes R_max).

    Answer: d. R/4

    09. What is the power required by a pump to draw 10 litres of water to a height of 10m, every second? [প্রতি সেকেন্ডে 10 লিটার জলকে 10 মিটার উচ্চতায় তুলতে একটি পাম্পের প্রয়োজনীয় শক্তি কত?]

    • 980 J
    • 100 kW
    • 980 W
    • 980 kW

    Solution:

    Power = Work done / Time.

    The pump does work against gravity to lift the water.

    Work done (W) = Potential energy gained = mgh.

    Volume of water per second = 10 litres = 10 × 10-3 m3 (since 1 litre = 10-3 m3).

    Density of water (\(\rho\)) ≈ 1000 kg/m3.

    Mass of water lifted per second (m/t) = Volume per second × Density

    m/t = (10 × 10-3 m3/s) × (1000 kg/m3) = 10 kg/s.

    Height (h) = 10 m.

    Gravitational acceleration (g) ≈ 9.8 m/s2.

    Power = Work / Time = (mgh) / t = (m/t) × g × h.

    Power = (10 kg/s) × (9.8 m/s2) × (10 m)

    Power = 10 × 9.8 × 10 = 980 Watts (W).

    (Note: Option a is in Joules, which is energy/work, not power).

    Answer: c. 980 W

    10. Two satellites share the same orbit and one of them has more mass than the other. Heavier satellite's rotation period is how many times than the lighter one? [দুটি উপগ্রহ একই কক্ষপথ ভাগ করে এবং তাদের একটির ভর অন্যটির চেয়ে বেশি। ভারী উপগ্রহের ঘূর্ণনকাল হালকাটির চেয়ে কত গুণ?]

    • Equal
    • Half
    • Double
    • Four times

    Solution:

    Since both satellites share the same orbit, they have the same orbital radius (R) and orbit the same central body.

    Therefore, their orbital periods must be equal, regardless of their masses.
