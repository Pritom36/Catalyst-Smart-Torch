Section C – [General Math]

1. Srabon wants to save 25000 taka, on a compounding Interest of 10% for 10 year period, how much must he Invest now? [ শ্রাবণ 25000 টাকা সঞ্চয় করতে চায়, 10 বছরের জন্য 10% চক্রবৃদ্ধি সুদে, তাকে এখন কত টাকা বিনিয়োগ করতে হবে?]

  • 9605.52 Tk
  • 9638.58 Tk
  • 9672.34 Tk
  • 9645.46 Tk

Solution:

This requires the Present Value (P) formula for compound interest.

\( P = \frac{A}{(1 + r)^n} \)

  • A = Future Amount = 25000 taka
  • r = Annual interest rate = 10% = 0.10
  • n = Number of years = 10

\( P = \frac{25000}{(1 + 0.10)^{10}} = \frac{25000}{(1.1)^{10}} \)

From previous calculation (Page 199 Q1), \( (1.1)^{10} \approx 2.59374 \).

\( P = \frac{25000}{2.59374} \approx 9638.58 \) taka.

Answer: b. 9638.58 Tk

2. Subha goes downstream with a boat to her original place in 6 hours. If the speed of the boat in still water and the stream are 12 km/h and 5 km/h respectively, the distance of the destination from the starting place Is: [শুভা একটি নৌকা নিয়ে স্রোতের অনুকূলে 6 ঘন্টায় তার আসল জায়গায় ফিরে যায়। যদি স্থির পানিতে নৌকা এবং স্রোতের বেগ যথাক্রমে 12 কিমি/ঘন্টা ও 5 কিমি/ঘন্টা হয়, তাহলে শুরু স্থান থেকে গন্তব্যের দূরত্ব কত?] (Question seems contradictory - goes downstream *to* her original place? Assume goes downstream *and returns* in 6 hours total).

  • 16.75 km
  • 29.75 km
  • 23.74 km
  • 28.35 km

Solution:

Assuming the question means Subha travels downstream to a destination and returns upstream to the original place, with the total journey taking 6 hours.

Speed of boat in still water (Sb) = 12 km/h.

Speed of stream (Ss) = 5 km/h.

Speed downstream (Sd) = Sb + Ss = 12 + 5 = 17 km/h.

Speed upstream (Su) = Sb - Ss = 12 - 5 = 7 km/h.

Let the distance one way be D km.

Time downstream (Td) = D / Sd = D / 17.

Time upstream (Tu) = D / Su = D / 7.

Total time = Td + Tu = 6 hours.

\( \frac{D}{17} + \frac{D}{7} = 6 \)

Common denominator = 17 × 7 = 119.

\( \frac{7D}{119} + \frac{17D}{119} = 6 \)

\( \frac{24D}{119} = 6 \)

\( 24D = 6 \times 119 \)

\( 4D = 119 \)

\( D = 119 / 4 = 29.75 \) km.

Answer: b. 29.75 km

3. The average mother's, father's, and son's age is 23 years. The average mother's and son's age is 25 years. What is the father's age? [মা, বাবা, ও ছেলের গড় বয়স 23 বছর। মা ও ছেলের গড় বয়স 25 বছর। বাবার বয়স কত?]

  • 9 years
  • 11 years
  • 10 years
  • 12 years

Solution:

Let M, F, S be the ages of Mother, Father, Son.

Given 1: Average(M, F, S) = 23.

\( \frac{M + F + S}{3} = 23 \implies M + F + S = 23 \times 3 = 69 \). (Eq 1)

Given 2: Average(M, S) = 25.

\( \frac{M + S}{2} = 25 \implies M + S = 25 \times 2 = 50 \). (Eq 2)

We want to find the Father's age, F.

Substitute (M + S) = 50 from Eq 2 into Eq 1:

\( F + (M + S) = 69 \)

\( F + 50 = 69 \)

\( F = 69 - 50 = 19 \).

The father's age is 19 years. None of the options match.

Answer: (Correct answer 19 years, not in options)

4. A jar contains 10 red marbles, 6 blue marbles, and 4 green marbles. If two marbles are drawn without replacement, what is the probability of drawing two red marbles? [একটি জারে 10টি লাল মার্বেল, 6টি নীল মার্বেল এবং 4টি সবুজ মার্বেল রয়েছে। যদি প্রতিস্থাপন ছাড়া দুটি মার্বেল টানা হয়, তাহলে দুটি লাল মার্বেল আঁকার সম্ভাবনা কত?]

  • 1/15
  • 9/38
  • 1/30
  • 1/45

Solution:

Total number of marbles = 10 (red) + 6 (blue) + 4 (green) = 20 marbles.

Probability of drawing the first red marble = (Number of red) / (Total) = 10 / 20 = 1/2.

After drawing one red marble without replacement:

Number of red marbles remaining = 9.

Total number of marbles remaining = 19.

Probability of drawing the second red marble = (Number of red remaining) / (Total remaining) = 9 / 19.

Probability of drawing two red marbles in a row = P(1st is Red) × P(2nd is Red | 1st was Red)

Probability = \( \frac{10}{20} \times \frac{9}{19} = \frac{1}{2} \times \frac{9}{19} = \frac{9}{38} \).

Answer: b. 9/38

5. A man sells a shirt for 2000 taka after giving a 10% discount, if he would sell the shirt at the original price he would have made a 50% profit, how much profit in percentage did he make after discount? [ একজন লোক 10% ডিসকাউন্ট দেওয়ার পর 2000 টাকায় একটি শার্ট বিক্রি করে, যদি সে আসল দামে শার্টটি বিক্রি করত তাহলে তার 50% লাভ হত, ডিসকাউন্টের পরে সে শতকরা কত লাভ করেছে?]

  • 1560
  • 1568.56
  • 1447.68
  • 1481.48

Solution:

Let the Original Price (Marked Price) be MP and Cost Price be CP.

Selling Price after 10% discount (SP_actual) = 2000 taka.

SP_actual = MP - 10% of MP = 0.90 MP.

2000 = 0.90 MP

MP = 2000 / 0.90 = 20000 / 9 taka.

If sold at original price (MP), profit would be 50%.

MP = CP + 50% of CP = 1.50 CP = (3/2) CP.

So, \( \frac{20000}{9} = \frac{3}{2} CP \)

\( CP = \frac{20000}{9} \times \frac{2}{3} = \frac{40000}{27} \) taka ≈ 1481.48 taka.

Now find the profit percentage made with the actual selling price (2000 taka).

Actual Profit = SP_actual - CP = 2000 - (40000 / 27)

Actual Profit = \( \frac{54000 - 40000}{27} = \frac{14000}{27} \).

Profit Percentage = \( \frac{\text{Actual Profit}}{CP} \times 100 = \frac{14000 / 27}{40000 / 27} \times 100 \)

Profit Percentage = \( \frac{14000}{40000} \times 100 = \frac{14}{40} \times 100 = \frac{7}{20} \times 100 = 7 \times 5 = 35\% \).

Let's recheck. MP = 20000/9. CP = MP / 1.5 = (20000/9) / (3/2) = (20000/9) * (2/3) = 40000/27. SP_actual = 2000. Profit = 2000 - 40000/27 = (54000-40000)/27 = 14000/27. Profit % = (Profit/CP)*100 = ((14000/27)/(40000/27))*100 = (14000/40000)*100 = (14/40)*100 = 35%.

None of the options match 35%. The options look like amounts, not percentages. Option d matches the CP calculation.

Answer: (Calculation gives 35% profit, does not match options which seem to be amounts)

6. A movie was screened for 3 days- Monday, Tuesday, and Wednesday. The respective ratio between the number of spectators on Monday, Tuesday and Wednesday was 2:3:5 and the price charged for three days was in the respective ration 2:3:4. If the difference between the amount earned in Tuesday and Wednesday was Tk.8800. What was the total amount earned in all three days? [ একটি চলচ্চিত্র 3 দিন প্রদর্শিত হয়েছিল - সোমবার, মঙ্গলবার এবং বুধবার। সোমবার, মঙ্গলবার এবং বুধবার দর্শকের সংখ্যার মধ্যে নিজ নিজ অনুপাত ছিল 2:3:5 এবং তিন দিনের জন্য চার্জ করা মূল্য ছিল নিজ নিজ অনুপাত 2:3:4। যদি মঙ্গলবার এবং বুধবার অর্জিত অর্থের মধ্যে পার্থক্য 8800 টাকা হয়। তাহলে তিন দিনে মোট কত টাকা আয় হয়েছিল?]

  • 24800
  • 27500
  • 26400
  • 22820

Solution:

Ratio of Spectators (Mon : Tue : Wed) = 2x : 3x : 5x (Let x be the constant).

Ratio of Prices (Mon : Tue : Wed) = 2y : 3y : 4y (Let y be the constant).

Amount Earned = Number of Spectators × Price per Spectator.

Amount Mon = (2x)(2y) = 4xy.

Amount Tue = (3x)(3y) = 9xy.

Amount Wed = (5x)(4y) = 20xy.

Difference between amount earned on Wednesday and Tuesday = Amount Wed - Amount Tue.

Difference = 20xy - 9xy = 11xy.

Given Difference = 8800 Tk.

11xy = 8800.

xy = 8800 / 11 = 800.

Total amount earned in three days = Amount Mon + Amount Tue + Amount Wed.

Total Amount = 4xy + 9xy + 20xy = 33xy.

Substitute xy = 800:

Total Amount = 33 × 800 = 26400 Tk.

Answer: c. 26400

7. If cosx.cosec23° = 1, the value of x is?

  • 23
  • 37
  • 63
  • 67

Solution:

\( \cos x \cdot \csc 23^\circ = 1 \)

Recall \( \csc \theta = 1 / \sin \theta \).

\( \cos x \cdot \frac{1}{\sin 23^\circ} = 1 \)

\( \cos x = \sin 23^\circ \)

Use the co-function identity \( \sin \theta = \cos(90^\circ - \theta) \).

\( \sin 23^\circ = \cos(90^\circ - 23^\circ) = \cos(67^\circ) \).

8. The sum of the three prime numbers [মৌলিক সংখ্যা] is 100. If one of them exceeds another by 36 [ একটি আরেকটি থেকে 36 বেশি], then one of the numbers is

  • 7
  • 29
  • 41
  • 67

Solution:

Let the three prime numbers be p1, p2, p3.

Sum = p1 + p2 + p3 = 100.

The sum (100) is an even number. The sum of three numbers is even if either all three are even OR one is even and two are odd.

The only even prime number is 2.

Case 1: All three are 2. Sum = 2+2+2 = 6 ≠ 100. Impossible.

Case 2: One is 2, the other two are odd primes. Let p1 = 2.

Then \( 2 + p2 + p3 = 100 \implies p2 + p3 = 98 \).

We are also given that one prime exceeds another by 36. Since p2 and p3 are the odd primes, let this relationship be between them: \( p3 = p2 + 36 \) (assuming p3 > p2).

Substitute this into \( p2 + p3 = 98 \):

\( p2 + (p2 + 36) = 98 \)

\( 2 \times p2 + 36 = 98 \)

\( 2 \times p2 = 98 - 36 = 62 \)

\( p2 = 31 \).

Check if p2=31 is prime. Yes.

Find p3: \( p3 = p2 + 36 = 31 + 36 = 67 \).

Check if p3=67 is prime. Check divisors up to √67 ≈ 8.1 (2, 3, 5, 7). Not div by 2, 3 (6+7=13), 5. 67/7 ≈ 9.5. Yes, 67 is prime.

The three primes are 2, 31, 67.

The question asks for "one of the numbers". Check options:

Option d is 67, which is one of the primes.

Answer: d. 67

9. A pump can fill a tank in 6 hours. Due to a leakage, it takes an additional 2 hours to fill the tank. How long will the leakage take to empty the entire tank? [ একটি পাম্প 6 ঘন্টায় একটি ট্যাঙ্ক পূর্ণ করতে পারে। একটি ছিদ্রের কারণে, ট্যাঙ্কটি পূর্ণ করতে অতিরিক্ত 2 ঘন্টা সময় লাগে। ছিদ্রটি পুরো ট্যাঙ্কটি খালি করতে কত সময় নেবে?]

  • 12 hours
  • 18 hours
  • 24 hours
  • 36 hours

Solution:

Let the rate of the filling pump be \(R_{fill}\) (tank/hour).

Time taken by pump alone = 6 hours.

\(R_{fill} = 1/6\) tank/hour.

Let the rate of the leakage be \(R_{leak}\) (tank/hour). This is an outflow rate.

With leakage, time taken = 6 hours + 2 hours = 8 hours.

When both are operating, the net filling rate = \(R_{fill} - R_{leak}\).

Net rate = 1 / (Time taken with leakage) = 1/8 tank/hour.

So, \( R_{fill} - R_{leak} = 1/8 \).

\( 1/6 - R_{leak} = 1/8 \).

\( R_{leak} = 1/6 - 1/8 \).

Common denominator = 24.

\( R_{leak} = 4/24 - 3/24 = 1/24 \) tank/hour.

This is the rate at which the leak empties the tank.

Time taken for the leak alone to empty the entire tank = Work / Rate = 1 / \(R_{leak}\).

Time = 1 / (1/24) = 24 hours.

Answer: c. 24 hours

01. If y = x4 - 4x3 + 5 then what will be the minimum value [ লঘুমান]?

  • 10
  • -22
  • 15
  • 20

Solution:

To find minimum/maximum values, find the first derivative and set it to zero.

\( y = x^4 - 4x^3 + 5 \)

\( \frac{dy}{dx} = 4x^3 - 12x^2 \)

Set \( \frac{dy}{dx} = 0 \):

\( 4x^3 - 12x^2 = 0 \)

\( 4x^2(x - 3) = 0 \)

Critical points are x = 0 and x = 3.

Now find the second derivative to classify these points.

\( \frac{d^2y}{dx^2} = \frac{d}{dx}(4x^3 - 12x^2) = 12x^2 - 24x \).

Check x = 0: \( \frac{d^2y}{dx^2} = 12(0)^2 - 24(0) = 0 \). The second derivative test is inconclusive. We need to check the sign of the first derivative around x=0. For x slightly < 0 (e.g., -0.1), \( \frac{dy}{dx} = 4(-0.1)^3 - 12(-0.1)^2 = 4(-0.001) - 12(0.01) = -0.004 - 0.12 < 0 \). For x slightly > 0 (e.g., 0.1), \( \frac{dy}{dx} = 4(0.1)^3 - 12(0.1)^2 = 0.004 - 0.12 < 0 \). Since the derivative doesn't change sign from negative to positive, x=0 is not a local minimum (it's an inflection point).

Check x = 3: \( \frac{d^2y}{dx^2} = 12(3)^2 - 24(3) = 12(9) - 72 = 108 - 72 = 36 \). Since the second derivative is positive (> 0), x = 3 corresponds to a local minimum.

Find the minimum value by substituting x = 3 into the original function:

\( y_{min} = (3)^4 - 4(3)^3 + 5 \)

\( y_{min} = 81 - 4(27) + 5 \)

\( y_{min} = 81 - 108 + 5 = 86 - 108 = -22 \).

The minimum value is -22.

Answer: b. -22

02. What is the value of \( \int_{0}^{\pi} \sin^2 2\theta \, d\theta \)?

  • π/2
  • π/3
  • 1
  • π/4

Solution:

Use the power-reduction identity: \( \sin^2 A = \frac{1 - \cos(2A)}{2} \).

Here, \( A = 2\theta \), so \( 2A = 4\theta \).

\( \sin^2 2\theta = \frac{1 - \cos(4\theta)}{2} \).

The integral becomes:

\[ I = \int_{0}^{\pi} \frac{1 - \cos(4\theta)}{2} \, d\theta = \frac{1}{2} \int_{0}^{\pi} (1 - \cos(4\theta)) \, d\theta \]

Integrate term by term:

\[ I = \frac{1}{2} \left[ \theta - \frac{\sin(4\theta)}{4} \right]_0^{\pi} \]

Evaluate at limits:

Upper limit (\(\theta = \pi\)): \( \pi - \frac{\sin(4\pi)}{4} = \pi - \frac{0}{4} = \pi \).

Lower limit (\(\theta = 0\)): \( 0 - \frac{\sin(0)}{4} = 0 - 0 = 0 \).

Value = (Value at upper limit) - (Value at lower limit)

\[ I = \frac{1}{2} [ (\pi) - (0) ] = \frac{\pi}{2} \]

Answer: a. π/2

03. Vertex of a parabola (-3, 3) and equation of directrix 4x + 15 = 0. What will be the equation of the axis? [পরাবৃত্তের শীর্ষবিন্দু (-3, 3) এবং নিয়ামক রেখার সমীকরণ 4x + 15 = 0। অক্ষের সমীকরণ কী হবে?]

  • y - 3 = 0
  • y = 0
  • y = 2

Solution:

The axis of a parabola is a line that passes through the vertex and is perpendicular to the directrix.

Vertex = (-3, 3).

Directrix equation: \( 4x + 15 = 0 \), which simplifies to \( x = -15/4 = -3.75 \).

The directrix \(x = -3.75\) is a vertical line.

The equation of the axis is y = 3.

04. The roots of the equation x2 + 3x + 5 = 0 [x2 + 3x + 5 = 0 সমীকরণের মূলগুলো-]

  • Real and unequal
  • Real Equal
  • Complex, equal
  • Complex, unequal

Solution:

To determine the nature of the roots of a quadratic equation \(ax^2 + bx + c = 0\), we examine the discriminant (\(\Delta\)).

\( \Delta = b^2 - 4ac \)

In the equation \(x^2 + 3x + 5 = 0\):

a = 1, b = 3, c = 5.

\( \Delta = (3)^2 - 4(1)(5) = 9 - 20 = -11 \).

Since the discriminant \(\Delta\) is negative (\(\Delta < 0\)), the roots are complex and unequal (they will be complex conjugates).

05. Under the condition f : R -> R which one of the following is not true for f(x) = x3+3? [f : R -> R শর্তের অধীনে f(x) = x3+3 এর জন্য নিচের কোনটি সত্য নয়?]

  • one-one function
  • Not one-one function
  • on-to function
  • None

Solution:

Let \( f(x) = x^3 + 3 \), where the domain and codomain are R (real numbers).

1. Check if one-one (injective):

Assume \( f(x_1) = f(x_2) \).

\( x_1^3 + 3 = x_2^3 + 3 \)

\( x_1^3 = x_2^3 \).

Taking the cube root (which is unique for real numbers) gives \( x_1 = x_2 \).

Therefore, the function is one-one.

2. Check if onto (surjective):

For a function to be onto, for every y in the codomain (R), there must exist an x in the domain (R) such that f(x) = y.

\( y = x^3 + 3 \)

\( y - 3 = x^3 \)

\( x = \sqrt[3]{y - 3} \).

For any real number y, \(y-3\) is also a real number, and the cube root of any real number is a real number. So, for every y in R, there exists a corresponding x in R.

Therefore, the function is onto.

The function is both one-one and onto.

The question asks which statement is *not true*.

Option a (one-one function) is true.

Option b (Not one-one function) is false.

Option c (on-to function) is true.

The statement that is not true is "Not one-one function".

Answer: b. Not one-one function

06. At which depth of earth, the value of g will be ½ of the value of g on Earth's surface? [পৃথিবীর কোন গভীরতায়, g এর মান পৃথিবীর পৃষ্ঠের g এর মানের ½ হবে?]

  • 51.2 x103 km
  • 5.12 x 103 km

Acceleration due to gravity at depth d below the surface (\(g_d\)) is related to the surface gravity (g) and Earth's radius (R) by:

\( g_d = g \left( 1 - \frac{d}{R} \right) \)

We want the depth 'd' where \( g_d = \frac{1}{2} g \).

\( \frac{1}{2} g = g \left( 1 - \frac{d}{R} \right) \)

Cancel g:

\( \frac{1}{2} = 1 - \frac{d}{R} \)

\( \frac{d}{R} = 1 - \frac{1}{2} = \frac{1}{2} \)

\( d = \frac{R}{2} \).

The depth required is half the Earth's radius.

Approximate Radius of Earth (R) ≈ 6400 km = 6.4 × 103 km = 6.4 × 106 m.

Depth d = R / 2 ≈ 6400 km / 2 = 3200 km.

Convert 3200 km to the format in options:

3200 km = 3.2 × 103 km.

Option d (3.12x103 km) is the closest.

  • 1.8 m/s
  • 2.4 m/s
  • 3.0 m/s

Solution:

In a perfectly elastic collision between two objects of equal mass, they simply exchange their velocities.

Let mass \(m_1 = m_2 = 5\) kg.

Initial velocity of object 1, \(u_1 = +3\) m/s.

Initial velocity of object 2, \(u_2 = -2\) m/s (opposite direction).

After the collision, the final velocities (\(v_1, v_2\)) will be:

Final velocity of object 1, \(v_1 = u_2 = -2\) m/s.

The final velocities are -2 m/s and 3 m/s. None of the options reflect these values. The options only give single values.

10. When will a person feel weightless on a lift? [কখন একজন ব্যক্তি লিফটে ওজনহীন বোধ করবে?]

  • If lift goes up at a uniform velocity
  • Goes down at at uniform velocity
  • Goes up at g acceleration
  • Goes down at g acceleration

Solution:

Apparent weight (\(W_{app}\)) is the normal force exerted on the person, which is what they feel as weight.

If accelerating upwards with 'a': \(W_{app} = m(g+a)\).

If accelerating downwards with 'a': \(W_{app} = m(g-a)\).

If moving at uniform velocity (a=0): \(W_{app} = mg\) (normal weight).

Weightlessness occurs when the apparent weight is zero (\(W_{app} = 0\)).

This happens when \( m(g-a) = 0 \), which requires \( a = g \).

So, a person feels weightless if the lift is accelerating downwards with acceleration equal to g (freefall).

Check options:

  • a & b: Uniform velocity means a=0, apparent weight = normal weight.
  • c: Goes up at g acceleration: \(W_{app} = m(g+g) = 2mg\). Feels twice as heavy.
  • d: Goes down at g acceleration: \(W_{app} = m(g-g) = 0\). Feels weightless.

Answer: d. Goes down at g acceleration
