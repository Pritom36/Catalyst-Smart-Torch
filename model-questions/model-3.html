  • 2500 m\(^2\)
  • 3000 m\(^2\)
  • 3500 m\(^2\)
  • 4000 m\(^2\)

Perimeter = \(10x + 24x + 26x = 60x\) meters.

Given perimeter = 300 meters.

So, \(60x = 300 \implies x = \frac{300}{60} = 5\) meters.

The side lengths are \(10 \times 5 = 50\) m, \(24 \times 5 = 120\) m, and \(26 \times 5 = 130\) m.

Check if it's a right-angled triangle: \(50^2 + 120^2 = 2500 + 14400 = 16900\), and \(130^2 = 16900\). Since \(50^2 + 120^2 = 130^2\), it is a right-angled triangle with base = 50 m and height = 120 m (or vice versa).

Area of the triangle = \(\frac{1}{2} \times \text{base} \times \text{height} = \frac{1}{2} \times 50 \times 120 = 25 \times 120 = 3000\) m\(^2\).

4. The population of town A and town B were the same. From 1985 to 1995 the population of town A increased by 60% while the population of town B decreased by 60%. What percentage of the population of town A was the population of town B in 1995? [A এবং B শহরের জনসংখ্যা সমান ছিল। ১৯৮৫ থেকে ১৯৯৫ পর্যন্ত A শহরের জনসংখ্যা বৃদ্ধি পেল ৬০% যেখানে B শহরের জনসংখ্যা হ্রাস পেল ৬০%। ১৯৯৫ সালে A শহরের জনসংখ্যার কত শতাংশ ছিল B শহরের জনসংখ্যা?]

Options:

  • 60
  • 75
  • 25
  • 45

Solution: Let the population of both towns in 1985 be \(P\).

In 1995, the population of town A increased by 60%, so the new population of A is \(P + 0.60P = 1.60P\).

In 1995, the population of town B decreased by 60%, so the new population of B is \(P - 0.60P = 0.40P\).

We need to find what percentage of the population of town A was the population of town B in 1995.

Percentage = \(\frac{\text{Population of B in 1995}}{\text{Population of A in 1995}} \times 100\%\).

Percentage = \(\frac{0.40P}{1.60P} \times 100\% = \frac{0.40}{1.60} \times 100\% = \frac{4}{16} \times 100\% = \frac{1}{4} \times 100\% = 25\%\).

Answer: c. 25

6. Srabon can complete a certain journey by a car. If he covered 30% of the distance at the speed of 20km/h, 60% of the distance at 40km/h and the remaining of the distance at 10 km/h, his average speed is: [শ্রাবণ একটি গাড়িতে একটি যাত্রা সম্পন্ন করে। সে যদি মোট দূরত্বের ৩০% ২০ কিমি/ঘণ্টা গতিতে, ৬০% ৪০ কিমি/ঘণ্টা গতিতে এবং বাকি দূরত্ব ১০ কিমি/ঘণ্টা গতিতে অতিক্রম করে তাহলে তার গড় গতি কত?]

Options:

  • 25 km/h
  • 28 km/h
  • 30 km/h
  • 33 km/h

Solution: Let the total distance of the journey be \(D\) km.

Distance covered at 20 km/h = \(0.30D\). Time taken = \(\frac{0.30D}{20} = 0.015D\) hours.

Distance covered at 40 km/h = \(0.60D\). Time taken = \(\frac{0.60D}{40} = 0.015D\) hours.

Remaining distance = \(D - 0.30D - 0.60D = 0.10D\). Time taken = \(\frac{0.10D}{10} = 0.010D\) hours.

Total distance = \(D\).

Total time taken = \(0.015D + 0.015D + 0.010D = 0.040D\) hours.

Average speed = \(\frac{\text{Total distance}}{\text{Total time}} = \frac{D}{0.040D} = \frac{1}{0.040} = \frac{1000}{40} = 25\) km/h.

Answer: a. 25 km/h

07. From two places, 60 km apart, Srabon and Subha start towards each other at the same time and meet each other after 6 hour. If Srabon traveled with 2/3 of his speed and Subha traveled with double of his speed, they would have met after 5 hours. The speed of Srabon is:
[ ৬০ কিমি দূরত্বের দুইটি স্থান থেকে শ্রাবণ ও শুভ একজন আরেকজনের দিকে একই সময়ে যাত্রা শুরু করে এবং ৬ ঘণ্টা পর একজন আরেকজনকে দেখে । যদি শ্রাবণ নিজের বেগের ২/৩ অংশ বেগে এবং শুভ নিজের বেগের দ্বিগুণ বেগে যাত্রা শুরু করত , তাহলে তারা ৫ ঘণ্টা পর দেখা করতে পারত । শ্রাবণের বেগ কত ? ]

Options:

  • 4 km/h
  • 6 km/h
  • 10 km/h
  • 12 km/h

Solution:

Let Srabon's speed be \( S \) km/h and Subha's speed be \( H \) km/h.

Case 1: They travel towards each other, distance = 60 km, time = 6 hours. The relative speed is \( S + H \).

\[ \text{Distance} = \text{Relative Speed} \times \text{Time} \] \[ 60 = (S + H) \times 6 \] \[ S + H = \frac{60}{6} = 10 \quad \text{(Equation 1)} \]

Case 2: Srabon's speed is \( \frac{2}{3}S \), Subha's speed is \( 2H \). Time = 5 hours. The relative speed is \( \frac{2}{3}S + 2H \).

\[ 60 = \left(\frac{2}{3}S + 2H\right) \times 5 \] \[ \frac{2}{3}S + 2H = \frac{60}{5} = 12 \quad \text{(Equation 2)} \]

From Equation 1, \( H = 10 - S \). Substitute this into Equation 2:

\[ \frac{2}{3}S + 2(10 - S) = 12 \] \[ \frac{2}{3}S + 20 - 2S = 12 \] \[ 20 - 12 = 2S - \frac{2}{3}S \] \[ 8 = \frac{6S - 2S}{3} = \frac{4S}{3} \] \[ 4S = 8 \times 3 = 24 \] \[ S = \frac{24}{4} = 6 \]

So, Srabon's speed is 6 km/h.

Answer: b. 6 km/h

  • \( 3x^2 + 3\sqrt{2}x + 1 \)

Solution:

Let the zeroes of the quadratic polynomial be \( \alpha \) and \( \beta \).

Given: Sum of zeroes, \( \alpha + \beta = \sqrt{2} \).

Given: Product of zeroes, \( \alpha \beta = \frac{1}{3} \).

A general form of a quadratic polynomial with zeroes \( \alpha \) and \( \beta \) is:

\[ k(x^2 - (\text{Sum of zeroes})x + (\text{Product of zeroes})) \] \[ k(x^2 - (\alpha + \beta)x + \alpha \beta) \]

Substitute the given values:

\[ k\left(x^2 - \sqrt{2}x + \frac{1}{3}\right) \]

Calculate the combined work rate of B and C per day:

11. If A and B are in the ratio 3 : 4, and B and C in the ratio 12 : 13, then A and C will be in the ratio:
[ A , B এর অনুপাত 3 : 4, B , C এর অনুপাত 12 : 13 , A , C এর অনুপাত কত হবে ? ]

Options:

  • 3:13
  • 9:13
  • 36:13
  • 13:9

Solution:

Given ratios:

\( A : B = 3 : 4 \)

\( B : C = 12 : 13 \)

To find the ratio \( A : C \), we need to make the value corresponding to B the same in both ratios. The value for B is 4 in the first ratio and 12 in the second.

The least common multiple (LCM) of 4 and 12 is 12.

Multiply the first ratio by \( \frac{12}{4} = 3 \):

\( A : B = (3 \times 3) : (4 \times 3) = 9 : 12 \)

The second ratio is already \( B : C = 12 : 13 \).

Now we can combine the ratios: \( A : B : C = 9 : 12 : 13 \).

From this combined ratio, the ratio \( A : C \) is \( 9 : 13 \).

Answer: b. 9:13

[ A , B , C এর বেতনের অনুপাত যথাক্রমে ১ : ৩ : ৪ । যদি তাদের বেতন যথাক্রমে ৫ % , ১০ % ও ১৫ % হারে বৃদ্ধি পায় , তাহলে বেতনের অনুপাত দাঁড়াবে- ]

Options:

  • 20:66:95
  • 21:66:96
  • 21:66:92
  • 19:66:92

Solution:

Let the initial salaries of A, B, and C be \( k, 3k, 4k \) respectively, based on the ratio 1 : 3 : 4.

Calculate the increased salaries:

A's new salary = \( k + 5\% \text{ of } k = k + 0.05k = 1.05k \)

B's new salary = \( 3k + 10\% \text{ of } 3k = 3k + 0.10(3k) = 3k + 0.3k = 3.3k \)

C's new salary = \( 4k + 15\% \text{ of } 4k = 4k + 0.15(4k) = 4k + 0.6k = 4.6k \)

The ratio of the new salaries is \( 1.05k : 3.3k : 4.6k \).

We can remove the common factor \( k \): \( 1.05 : 3.3 : 4.6 \)

To get rid of the decimals, multiply all parts by 100 (or 20):

\( 1.05 \times 100 : 3.3 \times 100 : 4.6 \times 100 = 105 : 330 : 460 \)

Now, simplify the ratio by dividing by the greatest common divisor (GCD). The GCD of 105, 330, and 460 is 5.

\( \frac{105}{5} : \frac{330}{5} : \frac{460}{5} = 21 : 66 : 92 \)

Answer: c. 21:66:92

15. If \( 5x + 9y = 5 \) and \( 125x^3 + 729y^3 = 120 \), then the value of the product [ গুণফল ] of x and y is?

Options:

  • 1/4
  • 1/9
  • 1/135
  • 1/30

Solution:

We are given:

1) \( 5x + 9y = 5 \)

2) \( 125x^3 + 729y^3 = 120 \). Notice that \( 125x^3 = (5x)^3 \) and \( 729y^3 = (9y)^3 \). So, \( (5x)^3 + (9y)^3 = 120 \).

We use the algebraic identity \( a^3 + b^3 = (a + b)(a^2 - ab + b^2) \).

Let \( a = 5x \) and \( b = 9y \). Substitute into the identity using the given equations:

\[ (5x)^3 + (9y)^3 = (5x + 9y)((5x)^2 - (5x)(9y) + (9y)^2) \] \[ 120 = (5)(25x^2 - 45xy + 81y^2) \]

Divide by 5:

\[ \frac{120}{5} = 25x^2 - 45xy + 81y^2 \] \[ 24 = 25x^2 - 45xy + 81y^2 \quad \text{(Equation 3)} \]

Now, square the first given equation: \( (5x + 9y)^2 = 5^2 \).

\[ (5x)^2 + 2(5x)(9y) + (9y)^2 = 25 \] \[ 25x^2 + 90xy + 81y^2 = 25 \quad \text{(Equation 4)} \]

Subtract Equation 3 from Equation 4:

\[ (25x^2 + 90xy + 81y^2) - (25x^2 - 45xy + 81y^2) = 25 - 24 \] \[ 25x^2 + 90xy + 81y^2 - 25x^2 + 45xy - 81y^2 = 1 \] \[ (90 + 45)xy = 1 \] \[ 135xy = 1 \] \[ xy = \frac{1}{135} \]

  • \( \frac{1}{x} \log_{10} e \)

\[ \frac{d}{dx} (\log_{10} x) = \frac{d}{dx} \left( \frac{\ln x}{\ln 10} \right) \] \[ = \frac{1}{\ln 10} \cdot \frac{d}{dx} (\ln x) \] \[ = \frac{1}{\ln 10} \cdot \frac{1}{x} = \frac{1}{x \ln 10} \]

06. A ball is thrown horizontally with an initial velocity of 10 m/s. It travels a distance of 20 m in 2 seconds. What is the average acceleration of the ball during the 2 seconds?
[ একটি বল অনুভূমিকভাবে 10 মি/সেকেন্ডের প্রাথমিক বেগ সহ নিক্ষেপ করা হয় । এটি 2 সেকেন্ডে 20 মিটার দূরত্ব অতিক্রম করে । 2 সেকেন্ডের সময় বলের গড় ত্বরণ কত ? ]

Options:

  • 15 m/s\(^2\)
  • 10 m/s\(^2\)
  • 5 m/s\(^2\)
  • 20 m/s\(^2\)

Solution:

Initial velocity: \( \vec{u} = (10, 0) \) m/s (horizontal speed 10 m/s, vertical speed 0 m/s).

Time \( t = 2 \) s.

Horizontal distance \( s_x = 20 \) m.

Horizontal motion: \( s_x = u_x t + \frac{1}{2} a_x t^2 \). \( 20 = 10(2) + \frac{1}{2} a_x (2^2) \implies 20 = 20 + 2a_x \implies a_x = 0 \). The horizontal velocity is constant.

Vertical motion: The only acceleration acting on the ball (neglecting air resistance) is gravity, \( a_y = g \), acting downwards. We usually take \( g \approx 9.8 \) m/s\(^2\). The options suggest using \( g = 10 \) m/s\(^2\).

Average acceleration \( \vec{a}_{avg} = \vec{a} = (0, g) \).

The magnitude of the average acceleration is \( |\vec{a}_{avg}| = \sqrt{0^2 + g^2} = g \).

Using \( g = 10 \) m/s\(^2\), the average acceleration magnitude is 10 m/s\(^2\).

07. The ratio of average radius of the orbit of earth and mars is 3 : 4. If earth has 365 days per year then how many days will make a year on mars?
[ পৃথিবী এবং মঙ্গলের কক্ষপথের গড় ব্যাসার্ধের অনুপাত 3 : 4 . পৃথিবীতে 365 দিনে এক বছর হলে মঙ্গলে কত দিনে এক বছর হবে ? ]

Options:

  • 552 Days
  • 662 Days
  • 772 Days
  • 562 Days

Solution:

Let \( r_E \) and \( r_M \) be the average orbital radii of Earth and Mars, and \( T_E \) and \( T_M \) be their orbital periods (years).

Given: \( \frac{r_E}{r_M} = \frac{3}{4} \), so \( \frac{r_M}{r_E} = \frac{4}{3} \).

Given: \( T_E = 365 \) days.

By Kepler's Third Law of Planetary Motion: \( T^2 \propto r^3 \), or \( \frac{T^2}{r^3} = \text{constant} \).

\[ \left( \frac{T_M}{T_E} \right)^2 = \left( \frac{r_M}{r_E} \right)^3 \] \[ \left( \frac{T_M}{365} \right)^2 = \left( \frac{4}{3} \right)^3 = \frac{4^3}{3^3} = \frac{64}{27} \] \[ \frac{T_M}{365} = \sqrt{\frac{64}{27}} = \frac{\sqrt{64}}{\sqrt{27}} = \frac{8}{3\sqrt{3}} \] \[ T_M = 365 \times \frac{8}{3\sqrt{3}} \]

Using \( \sqrt{3} \approx 1.732 \):

\[ T_M \approx 365 \times \frac{8}{3 \times 1.732} \approx 365 \times \frac{8}{5.196} \approx 365 \times 1.5396 \approx 561.97 \text{ days} \]

Rounding to the nearest whole number, \( T_M \approx 562 \) days.

Answer: d. 562 Days

08. A copper wire of resistance 100 \( \Omega \) is made 3 times by pulling. What will be the current resistance of the wire?
[ 100 \( \Omega \) রোধবিশিষ্ট একটু তামার তারকে টেনে তিনগুন লম্বা করা হলো । তারটির বর্তমান রোধ কত ? ]

  • 27 \( \Omega \)
  • 90 \( \Omega \)
  • 100 \( \Omega \)

Solution:

Let the initial resistance be \( R_1 = 100 \, \Omega \), initial length \( L_1 \), and initial cross-sectional area \( A_1 \).

The resistance is given by \( R = \rho \frac{L}{A} \), where \( \rho \) is the resistivity of copper.

When the wire is pulled, its length increases, and its cross-sectional area decreases, but the volume remains constant.

New length \( L_2 = 3 L_1 \).

Volume conservation: \( V_1 = V_2 \implies A_1 L_1 = A_2 L_2 \).

So, \( A_2 = A_1 \frac{L_1}{L_2} = A_1 \frac{L_1}{3 L_1} = \frac{A_1}{3} \).

The new resistance \( R_2 \) is:

\[ R_2 = \rho \frac{L_2}{A_2} = \rho \frac{3 L_1}{A_1 / 3} = \rho \frac{9 L_1}{A_1} = 9 \left( \rho \frac{L_1}{A_1} \right) = 9 R_1 \]

\[ R_2 = 9 \times 100 \, \Omega = 900 \, \Omega \]

Answer: None of the options match; the resistance should be 900 \( \Omega \).
