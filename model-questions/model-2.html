01.

Options:

  • literature
  • language
  • cultural

Solution:

The phrase "get a kick" is an idiom meaning to get excitement or pleasure. Reading English books helps learning (related to language) and can be enjoyable. However, "cultural" might also fit the idea of gaining something extra from reading literature. Given the options, "language" is a plausible, if imperfect, fit relating learning and the source material. Let's assume the intended meaning relates to the benefit gained. Option D in the image is marked.

Answer: d. cultural (Based on image marking, though 'language' is contextually arguable)

01. The salary of one person was 15,000 tk in December. In the next year he will get a promotion and his salary will be increased by 10%. What will be his salary in the next year?
[ ডিসেম্বরে একজনের মাসিক বেতন ছিল ১৫,০০০ টাকা । সামনের বছরে, সে পদোন্নতি লাভ করবে এবং তার মাসিক বেতন বৃদ্ধি পাবে ১০% হারে । তখন তার বেতন কত হবে? ]

Options:

  • 16,500
  • 10,000
  • 15,500
  • 18,200

Solution:

Initial salary = 15,000 tk.

Increase percentage = 10%.

Increase amount = 10% of 15,000 = \( \frac{10}{100} \times 15000 = 0.1 \times 15000 = 1500 \) tk.

New salary = Initial salary + Increase amount

New salary = 15,000 + 1500 = 16,500 tk.

Answer: a. 16,500

02. Saad spent 2500 tk on Eid shopping, 1000 tk on buying bus ticket and the remaining 65% he saved in his bank account. What is his total income?
[ সাদ ঈদের বাজারে ২৫০০ টাকা, বাসের টিকেট কেনার ১০০০ টাকা ব্যয় করল এবং বাকি থাকা ৬৫% নিজের ব্যাংক একাউন্টে জমা করল । তার মোট ইনকাম কত? ]

Options:

  • 20,000
  • 10,000
  • 15,000
  • 12,500

Solution:

Total amount spent = Shopping cost + Bus ticket cost

Total spent = 2500 tk + 1000 tk = 3500 tk.

Percentage saved = 65%.

This means the percentage spent = 100% - 65% = 35%.

The amount spent (3500 tk) represents 35% of the total income.

Let the total income be \( I \).

Then, \( 35\% \text{ of } I = 3500 \)

\[ \frac{35}{100} \times I = 3500 \] \[ I = \frac{3500 \times 100}{35} \] \[ I = 100 \times 100 = 10,000 \text{ tk} \]

Answer: b. 10,000

03. If 2, 0 is a solution of the linear equation 2x + 3y = k, then the value of k is?
[ 2x + 3y = k সমীকরণের সমাধান 2, 0 হলে k এর মান কত? ]

Options:

  • 4
  • 6
  • 5
  • 2

Solution:

The given linear equation is \( 2x + 3y = k \).

A solution (2, 0) means that \( x = 2 \) and \( y = 0 \) satisfy the equation.

Substitute these values into the equation:

\[ 2(2) + 3(0) = k \] \[ 4 + 0 = k \] \[ k = 4 \]

Answer: a. 4

04. If x : y = 3 : 5 and x - y = -2, then the value of x + y is?

Options:

  • 8
  • 2
  • 3
  • 5

Solution:

Given \( x : y = 3 : 5 \), which means \( \frac{x}{y} = \frac{3}{5} \). This implies \( 5x = 3y \), or \( 5x - 3y = 0 \).

Also given \( x - y = -2 \). From this, \( x = y - 2 \).

Substitute the expression for \( x \) into the first equation:

\[ 5(y - 2) - 3y = 0 \] \[ 5y - 10 - 3y = 0 \] \[ 2y - 10 = 0 \] \[ 2y = 10 \] \[ y = 5 \]

Now find \( x \) using \( x = y - 2 \):

\[ x = 5 - 2 = 3 \]

We need to find the value of \( x + y \):

\[ x + y = 3 + 5 = 8 \]

Answer: a. 8

05. Two trains for Mumbai leave Delhi at 6 am and 6:45 am and travel at 100 kmph and 136 km/h respectively. How many kilometers from Delhi will the two trains be together?
[ ১০০ কিমি/ঘন্টা এবং ১৩৬ কিমি/ঘন্টা বেগে দুইটি ট্রেন মুম্বাই থেকে দিল্লির উদ্দেশ্যে যথাক্রমে ৬:০০টা এবং ৬:৪৫ এ ছেড়ে যায়। তাহলে দিল্লি থেকে কত কিলোমিটার দূরে ট্রেন দুইটি একত্রিত হবে? ]

Options:

  • 262.4 km
  • 260 km
  • 283.33 km
  • 275 km

Solution:

Train 1 (T1): Starts at 6:00 am, speed \( v_1 = 100 \) km/h.

Train 2 (T2): Starts at 6:45 am, speed \( v_2 = 136 \) km/h.

T2 starts 45 minutes (0.75 hours) later than T1.

In the first 0.75 hours, T1 travels a distance of \( d_{start} = v_1 \times 0.75 = 100 \times 0.75 = 75 \) km.

Now, consider the situation from 6:45 am. T1 is 75 km ahead of T2, and T2 is faster than T1.

The relative speed of T2 with respect to T1 is \( v_{rel} = v_2 - v_1 = 136 - 100 = 36 \) km/h.

Time taken for T2 to catch up the 75 km gap = \( t_{catch} = \frac{\text{Distance gap}}{\text{Relative speed}} = \frac{75}{36} \) hours.

\[ t_{catch} = \frac{75}{36} = \frac{25}{12} \text{ hours} \]

The distance from Delhi where they meet is the distance T2 travels in this time:

\[ \text{Meeting Distance} = v_2 \times t_{catch} = 136 \times \frac{25}{12} \] \[ = \frac{136}{12} \times 25 = \frac{34}{3} \times 25 = \frac{850}{3} \] \[ \approx 283.33 \text{ km} \]

Answer: c. 283.33 km

06. A man takes 6 hours 15 minutes in walking a distance and riding back to starting place. He could walk both ways in 7 hours 45 minutes. The time taken by him to ride back both ways is:
[ একজন লোক একটি স্থানে যাওয়ার সময় হেঁটে যেতে এবং সেখান থেকে ফেরার সময়ে যানবহনে উঠে ফিরতে সর্বমোট ৬ ঘন্টা ১৫ মিনিট ব্যয় করে । আসা ও যাওয়া দুইক্ষেত্রেই যানবহন ব্যবহার করলে প্রয়োজনীয় সময়- ]

Options:

  • 4 hours
  • 4 hours 45 min.
  • 4 hours 30 min.
  • 5 hours

Solution:

Let \( t_w \) be the time taken to walk one way.

Let \( t_r \) be the time taken to ride one way.

Given: Time for (walk one way + ride one way) = 6 hours 15 minutes.

\( t_w + t_r = 6 \text{ hr } 15 \text{ min} = 6 + \frac{15}{60} = 6.25 \) hours. (Equation 1)

Given: Time for (walk both ways) = 7 hours 45 minutes.

\( t_w + t_w = 2t_w = 7 \text{ hr } 45 \text{ min} = 7 + \frac{45}{60} = 7.75 \) hours. (Equation 2)

From Equation 2, find the time to walk one way:

\[ t_w = \frac{7.75}{2} = 3.875 \text{ hours} \]

Substitute this value of \( t_w \) into Equation 1:

\[ 3.875 + t_r = 6.25 \] \[ t_r = 6.25 - 3.875 = 2.375 \text{ hours} \]

The question asks for the time taken to ride both ways, which is \( 2t_r \).

\[ 2t_r = 2 \times 2.375 = 4.75 \text{ hours} \]

Convert 0.75 hours to minutes: \( 0.75 \times 60 = 45 \) minutes.

So, the time to ride both ways is 4 hours and 45 minutes.

Answer: b. 4 hours 45 min.

07. If A = {2,3,5} and U be the set of prime factors of 210 then find A'.
[ যদি A={2,3,5} এবং U হল ২১০ এর মৌলিক গুণনীয়কসমূহের সেট। তাহলে নির্ণয় করো A' ]

Options:

  • {2,3,5}
  • {2,3,5,7}
  • {7}
  • \( \phi \)

Solution:

Given set \( A = \{2, 3, 5\} \).

The universal set U is the set of prime factors of 210.

First, find the prime factorization of 210:

\[ 210 = 10 \times 21 = (2 \times 5) \times (3 \times 7) \]

The prime factors are 2, 3, 5, and 7.

So, the universal set is \( U = \{2, 3, 5, 7\} \).

A' (the complement of A) contains all elements of U that are not in A.

\[ A' = U - A = \{2, 3, 5, 7\} - \{2, 3, 5\} = \{7\} \]

Answer: c. {7}

[ এ এবং B একত্রে কোনো কাজ ৩ দিনে শেষ করতে পারে । তারা একত্রে একটি কাজ শুরু করে কিন্তু ২দিন পর B কাজটি ছেড়ে চলে যায় । বাকি কাজ শেষ হতে যদি ২ দিন প্রয়োজন হয়, তাহলে B এক্ক কাজটি কতদিনে শেষ করতে পারবে? ]

Options:

  • 5 days
  • 6 days
  • 9 days
  • 10 days

Solution:

Let \( R_A \) and \( R_B \) be the rate of work of A and B respectively (work done per day).

Together, their combined rate is \( R_{A+B} = R_A + R_B \).

They complete the work in 3 days together, so \( R_{A+B} = \frac{1}{3} \) work/day.

They work together for 2 days. Work done = \( R_{A+B} \times 2 = \frac{1}{3} \times 2 = \frac{2}{3} \).

Remaining work = \( 1 - \frac{2}{3} = \frac{1}{3} \).

B leaves, and A completes the remaining work (\( \frac{1}{3} \)) in 2 more days.

So, A's rate \( R_A = \frac{\text{Work done by A}}{\text{Time taken by A}} = \frac{1/3}{2} = \frac{1}{6} \) work/day.

Now we can find B's rate: \( R_B = R_{A+B} - R_A = \frac{1}{3} - \frac{1}{6} = \frac{2}{6} - \frac{1}{6} = \frac{1}{6} \) work/day.

The time B alone would take to complete the work is \( T_B = \frac{1}{R_B} = \frac{1}{1/6} = 6 \) days.

Answer: b. 6 days

02. What is the value of \(\frac{d}{dx} \cos(\frac{\pi x}{180})\)? ( \(\frac{d}{dx} \cos(\frac{\pi x}{180})\)-এর মান কোনটি?)

  • \(\frac{\pi}{180} \sin(\frac{\pi x}{180})\)
  • \(\cos(\frac{\pi x}{180})\)
  • \(\sin(\frac{\pi x}{180})\)

Solution:

We need to find the derivative of \(y = \cos(\frac{\pi x}{180})\) with respect to \(x\).

Using the chain rule: \(\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{d}{dx} \cos(u) \cdot \frac{du}{dx}\), where \(u = \frac{\pi x}{180}\).

03. If \(\lim_{x \to 0} f(x) = 1\), then which one of the following is true for \(f(x)\) = ? [যদি \(\lim_{x \to 0} f(x) = 1\) হয়, তাহলে নিচের কোনটি \(f(x)\) = ?]

  • \(\frac{\tan(x)}{x}\)
  • \(\frac{\tan(x)}{sin(x)}\)

Solution:

We need to evaluate the limit of each function as \(x \to 0\).

  • a. \(\lim_{x \to 0} \cos(x) = \cos(0) = 1\).
  • b. \(\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin(x)}{x} = 1\) (standard limit).

05. Which of the following functions has an inverse that is also a function? [নিচের কোন ফাংশনটির বিপরীত ফাংশনও একটি ফাংশন?]

Options:

  • \(f(x) = x^2\)
  • \(f(x) = |x|\)
  • \(f(x) = 2x + 1\)
  • \(f(x) = 1/x\)

Solution:

A function has an inverse that is also a function if and only if it is one-to-one (injective), meaning that for every \(y\) in the range, there is exactly one \(x\) in the domain such that \(f(x) = y\). Graphically, this means the function passes the horizontal line test.

  • \(f(x) = x^2\) fails the horizontal line test (e.g., \(f(2) = 4\) and \(f(-2) = 4\)).
  • \(f(x) = |x|\) also fails the horizontal line test (e.g., \(f(2) = 2\) and \(f(-2) = 2\)).
  • \(f(x) = 2x + 1\) is a linear function with a non-zero slope, so it is one-to-one and has an inverse function \(f^{-1}(x) = \frac{x - 1}{2}\).
  • \(f(x) = 1/x\) is also one-to-one (its graph passes the horizontal line test) and its inverse is \(f^{-1}(x) = 1/x\).

Both \(f(x) = 2x + 1\) and \(f(x) = 1/x\) have inverses that are also functions.

Answer: c. \(f(x) = 2x + 1\) and d. \(f(x) = 1/x\) (Both options are correct)
