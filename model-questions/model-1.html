None of the options exactly match 111. Let's re-check the question and options. It's possible there's a slight rounding or typo in the options or the percentage intended.

02. The ratio 5:4 expressed as percentage is: [অনুপাত ৫:৪ কে শতকরায় প্রকাশ করলে হবে:]

03. A cricket bat costs tk. 3000. How much should this bat be sold to gain a profit of 20%? [একটি ক্রিকেট ব্যাটের ক্রয়মূল্য ৩০০০ টাকা। ২০% লাভের জন্য ব্যাটটির বিক্রয়মূল্য কত হওয়া উচিত?]

04. Two buses start from a bus terminal with a speed of 20 km/h at intervals of 10 minutes. What is the speed of a man coming from the opposite direction towards the bus terminal if he meets the buses at intervals of 8 minutes? [একটি বাস টার্মিনাল থেকে দুটি বাস 20 কিমি/ঘণ্টা বেগে 10 মিনিট অন্তর ছেড়ে যায়। বিপরীত দিক থেকে টার্মিনালের দিকে আসা এক ব্যক্তি যদি বাস দুটিকে 8 মিনিট অন্তর সাক্ষাৎ করে, তবে ঐ ব্যক্তির গতিবেগ কত?]

Options:

  • 3 km/h
  • 4 km/h
  • 5 km/h
  • 7 km/h

Solution: Let the speed of the buses be \(v_b = 20\) km/h and the speed of the man be \(v_m\) km/h.

The time interval between the start of two consecutive buses is 10 minutes = \(\frac{10}{60} = \frac{1}{6}\) hour.

The distance between two consecutive buses when the second bus starts is \(d = v_b \times \frac{1}{6} = 20 \times \frac{1}{6} = \frac{10}{3}\) km.

The relative speed of the man and the buses coming from the opposite direction is \(v_r = v_b + v_m = 20 + v_m\) km/h.

The man meets the buses at intervals of 8 minutes = \(\frac{8}{60} = \frac{2}{15}\) hour.

Using the formula distance = relative speed × time, we have:

\[ d = v_r \times t \]

\[ \frac{10}{3} = (20 + v_m) \times \frac{2}{15} \]

\[ \frac{10}{3} \times \frac{15}{2} = 20 + v_m \]

\[ 5 \times 5 = 20 + v_m \]

\[ 25 = 20 + v_m \]

\[ v_m = 25 - 20 = 5 \, \text{km/h} \]

Answer: c. 5 km/h

05. Walking ¾ of his normal speed, Rabi is 16 minutes late in reaching his office. The usual time taken by him to cover the distance between his home and office: [নিজের স্বাভাবিক গতিবেগের ¾ অংশ বেগে হাঁটার ফলে রবি অফিসে পৌঁছাতে ১৬ মিনিট দেরি করে। স্বাভাবিক গতিতে হাঁটলে তার অফিস ও বাসার মধ্যবর্তী দূরত্ব পার করতে প্রয়োজনীয় সময়:]

Options:

  • 48 min.
  • 60 min.
  • 42 min.
  • 62 min.

Solution: Let the usual speed of Rabi be \(v\) and the usual time taken be \(t\) minutes.

The distance between his home and office is \(d = v \times t\).

When he walks at ¾ of his normal speed, his speed is \(\frac{3}{4}v\).

The time taken at this speed is \(t' = \frac{d}{\frac{3}{4}v} = \frac{vt}{\frac{3}{4}v} = \frac{4}{3}t\) minutes.

He is 16 minutes late, so \(t' = t + 16\).

\[ \frac{4}{3}t = t + 16 \]

\[ \frac{4}{3}t - t = 16 \]

\[ \frac{4t - 3t}{3} = 16 \]

\[ \frac{t}{3} = 16 \]

\[ t = 16 \times 3 = 48 \, \text{minutes} \]

The usual time taken by him is 48 minutes.

Answer: a. 48 min.

06. What does A' denote when U is a universal set and A is a set? [যদি U সার্বজনীন সেট হয়, তাহলে A' এর অর্থ কী?]

Options:

  • A
  • \(\emptyset\)
  • U
  • \(U - A\)

Solution: In set theory, if U is a universal set and A is a subset of U, then A' (or \(A^c\)) denotes the complement of A with respect to U. The complement of A is the set of all elements in U that are not in A.

Mathematically, \(A' = U - A = \{x \in U \mid x \notin A\}\).

Answer: d. \(U - A\)

07. The zeroes of the quadratic polynomial \(x^2 - 2x - 8\) are: [\(x^2 - 2x - 8\) বহুপদীটির শূন্যগুলো হলো:]

Options:

  • (2, -4)
  • (4, -2)
  • (-2, -2)
  • (-4, -4)

Solution: To find the zeroes of the quadratic polynomial \(x^2 - 2x - 8\), we need to solve the equation \(x^2 - 2x - 8 = 0\).

We can factor the quadratic expression:

We need two numbers that multiply to -8 and add up to -2. These numbers are -4 and 2.

So, \(x^2 - 2x - 8 = (x - 4)(x + 2) = 0\).

This gives us two possible values for \(x\):

\(x - 4 = 0 \implies x = 4\)

\(x + 2 = 0 \implies x = -2\)

The zeroes of the polynomial are 4 and -2, which can be written as the pair (4, -2).

Answer: b. (4, -2)

08. If A and B can complete a piece of work together in 15 days and B alone in 20 days, in how many days can A alone complete the work? [A এবং B একত্রে একটি কাজ ১৫ দিনে এবং B একা ঐ কাজটি ২০ দিনে করতে পারে, তাহলে A একা ঐ কাজটি কত দিনে শেষ করতে পারবে?]

Options:

  • 60
  • 45
  • 40
  • 30

Solution: Let the total work be W.

Work done by A and B together in one day = \(\frac{W}{15}\).

Work done by B alone in one day = \(\frac{W}{20}\).

Work done by A alone in one day = (Work done by A and B together in one day) - (Work done by B alone in one day)

Work done by A alone in one day = \(\frac{W}{15} - \frac{W}{20}\).

To subtract these fractions, find a common denominator, which is 60.

Work done by A alone in one day = \(\frac{4W}{60} - \frac{3W}{60} = \frac{4W - 3W}{60} = \frac{W}{60}\).

If A does \(\frac{W}{60}\) work in one day, then the number of days A alone will take to complete the work W is \(\frac{W}{\frac{W}{60}} = W \times \frac{60}{W} = 60\) days.

Answer: a. 60

13. Javed invested 8,000 taka with a simple interest of 10%. What will be his total amount in 5 years? [জাভেদ ৮০০০ টাকা ১০% সরল সুদের হারে বিনিয়োগ করে। তার ৫ বছরের মোট কত টাকা হবে?]

Options:

  • 12000
  • 10800
  • 9600
  • 15000

Solution: Principal amount (P) = 8000 taka.

Rate of simple interest (R) = 10% per year.

Time period (T) = 5 years.

Simple Interest (SI) = \(\frac{P \times R \times T}{100} = \frac{8000 \times 10 \times 5}{100} = 80 \times 10 \times 5 = 4000\) taka.

Total amount = Principal + Simple Interest = 8000 + 4000 = 12000 taka.

Answer: a. 12000

14. In a \(\triangle\)ABC, right-angled at B, AB = 24 cm, BC = 7 cm. The value of tan C is: [\( \triangle \)ABC এ B সমকোণ, AB = ২৪ সেমি, BC = ৭ সেমি। tan C এর মান-]

Options:

  • 24/7
  • 20/7
  • 7/24

The tangent of angle C (tan C) is defined as the ratio of the length of the side opposite to angle C to the length of the side adjacent to angle C.

01. Evaluate (যোগজীকরণ করো): \(\int \cos 5x \, dx\)

Options:

  • \(\frac{1}{5} \sin 5x + c\)
  • \(5 \sin 5x + c\)
  • \(\sin 5x + c\)
  • \(\cos 5x + c\)

Solution:

We need to evaluate the integral \(\int \cos 5x \, dx\).

Let \(u = 5x\), then \(du = 5 \, dx\), so \(dx = \frac{1}{5} \, du\).

Substituting, we get:

\[ \int \cos 5x \, dx = \int \cos u \cdot \frac{1}{5} \, du = \frac{1}{5} \int \cos u \, du \]

\[ = \frac{1}{5} \sin u + c = \frac{1}{5} \sin(5x) + c \]

Answer: a. \(\frac{1}{5} \sin 5x + c\)

02. What is the value of \(\frac{d}{dx} \sin^2 x\)? (নিচের কোনটি \(\frac{d}{dx} \sin^2 x\)-এর মান?)

Options:

  • \(2x \sin(2x)\)
  • \(2 \sin(2x)\)
  • \(2 \sin(4x)\)
  • \(4x \sin(4x)\)

Solution:

We need to find the derivative of \(y = \sin^2 x\) with respect to \(x\).

Using the chain rule: \(\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{d}{dx} (\sin x)^2 = 2(\sin x) \cdot \frac{d}{dx}(\sin x)\)

\[ = 2 \sin x \cdot \cos x \]

Using the double angle identity \(\sin(2x) = 2 \sin x \cos x\), we have:

\[ \frac{d}{dx} \sin^2 x = \sin(2x) \]

None of the provided options directly match \(\sin(2x)\). Option b, \(2 \sin(2x)\), is incorrect. There seems to be an issue with the options provided.

Answer: None of the provided options are correct. The derivative is \(\sin(2x)\).

03. What is the value of \(\lim_{x \to \infty} (1 + \frac{1}{x})^{x+3}\)? ( \(\lim_{x \to \infty} (1 + \frac{1}{x})^{x+3}\)-এর মান কোনটি?)

Options:

  • \(e^3\)
  • \(e^2\)
  • \(e\)
  • \(1\)

Solution:

We need to evaluate the limit \(\lim_{x \to \infty} (1 + \frac{1}{x})^{x+3}\).

We can rewrite the expression as:

\[ \lim_{x \to \infty} (1 + \frac{1}{x})^{x+3} = \lim_{x \to \infty} (1 + \frac{1}{x})^{x} \cdot (1 + \frac{1}{x})^{3} \]

We know that \(\lim_{x \to \infty} (1 + \frac{1}{x})^{x} = e\).

Also, \(\lim_{x \to \infty} (1 + \frac{1}{x})^{3} = (1 + 0)^3 = 1^3 = 1\).

Therefore, the limit is:

\[ \lim_{x \to \infty} (1 + \frac{1}{x})^{x+3} = e \cdot 1 = e \]

Answer: c. \(e\)

04. What is the slope of the line \(4x + 5y = 0\)? ( \(4x + 5y = 0\) সরলরেখার ঢাল কোনটি?)

Options:

  • \(5\)
  • \(4\)
  • \(-4/5\)
  • \(5/4\)

Solution:

To find the slope, we rewrite the equation in the form \(y = mx + c\), where \(m\) is the slope.

Given \(4x + 5y = 0\).

Subtract \(4x\) from both sides: \(5y = -4x\).

Divide by 5: \(y = -\frac{4}{5}x + 0\).

The slope \(m = -\frac{4}{5}\).

Answer: c. \(-4/5\)

05. What is the inverse of the function \(f(x) = e^{2x}\)? ( \(f(x) = e^{2x}\) ফাংশনের বিপরীত ফাংশন কোনটি?)

Options:

  • \(\ln(x) / 2\)
  • \(\ln(x/2)\)
  • \(\ln(x) / 2e\)
  • \(\ln(x / 2e)\)

Solution:

Let \(y = f(x) = e^{2x}\).

To find the inverse, swap \(x\) and \(y\): \(x = e^{2y}\).

Take the natural logarithm of both sides: \(\ln x = \ln(e^{2y})\).

\(\ln x = 2y\).

Solve for \(y\): \(y = \frac{\ln x}{2}\).

So, the inverse function is \(f^{-1}(x) = \frac{\ln x}{2}\).

Answer: a. \(\ln(x) / 2\)

06. In Rutherford's Alpha Scattering Experiment, what was the thickness of the thin gold sheet? (রাদারফোর্ডের আলফা কণার বিচ্ছুরণ পরীক্ষায়, পাতলা সোনার পাতের পুরুত্ব কত ছিল?)

07. A man is walking on a road with a velocity 3 km/hr. Suddenly rain starts falling. The velocity of rain is 10 km/hr in a vertically downward direction. The relative velocity of the rain with respect to man is [যদি কোনো ব্যক্তি 3 কিমি/ঘণ্টা বেগে হেঁটে যায় এবং লম্বভাবে 10 কিমি/ঘণ্টা বেগে বৃষ্টি পড়ে, তবে ব্যক্তির সাপেক্ষে বৃষ্টির আপেক্ষিক বেগ কত?]

Options:

  • \(\sqrt{13} \, \text{km/hr}\)
  • \(\sqrt{7} \, \text{km/hr}\)
  • \(\sqrt{109} \, \text{km/hr}\)
  • \(\sqrt{103} \, \text{km/hr}\)

Solution:

Velocity of man \(\vec{v}_m = 3\hat{i}\) km/hr.

Velocity of rain \(\vec{v}_r = -10\hat{j}\) km/hr.

Relative velocity of rain with respect to man \(\vec{v}_{rm} = \vec{v}_r - \vec{v}_m = -10\hat{j} - 3\hat{i} = -3\hat{i} - 10\hat{j}\) km/hr.

Magnitude of relative velocity \(|\vec{v}_{rm}| = \sqrt{(-3)^2 + (-10)^2} = \sqrt{9 + 100} = \sqrt{109} \, \text{km/hr}\).

Answer: c. \(\sqrt{109} \, \text{km/hr}\)

08. A train is moving with a velocity of 20 m/s and increases its velocity to 40 m/s in 5 seconds. What is the average acceleration of the train? [একটি ট্রেন 20 মি/সেকেন্ড বেগে চলতে গিয়ে 5 সেকেন্ড পর তার বেগ বৃদ্ধি করে 40 মি/সেকেন্ডে উন্নীত হলো। ট্রেনটির গড় ত্বরণ কত?]

Options:

  • \(4 \, \text{m/s}^2\)
  • \(8 \, \text{m/s}^2\)
  • \(12 \, \text{m/s}^2\)
  • \(16 \, \text{m/s}^2\)

Solution:

Initial velocity \(u = 20\) m/s.

Final velocity \(v = 40\) m/s.

Time interval \(t = 5\) s.

Average acceleration \(a = \frac{v - u}{t} = \frac{40 - 20}{5} = \frac{20}{5} = 4 \, \text{m/s}^2\).

Answer: a. \(4 \, \text{m/s}^2\)

Diameter \(d = 400\) cm, so radius \(r = 200\) cm.

Linear velocity \(v = 1600\) cm/s.
