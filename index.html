How It Works

Catalyst Smart Torch helps you manage your device's flashlight with timer functionality:

  • Set hours, minutes, or seconds for automatic flashlight turn-off
  • Use presets for common time intervals (requires watching a short ad)
  • Pause/resume the timer as needed (requires watching a short ad)
  • Cancel anytime to turn off immediately (requires watching a short ad)

The app works best on Chrome for Android with devices that support flashlight functionality.

Note: Watching ads helps support the development of this free app.
